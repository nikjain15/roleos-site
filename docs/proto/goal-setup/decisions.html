Goal setup — design decisions

Status: APPROVED (2026-07-04) · PRD shipped: roleos-app PR #39 (stacked on #37) · prototype at /proto/goal-setup/ · becomes the formal PRD on APPROVED. The goal engine (funnel math, pace, adaptation) is already live in the app — this designs the experience.

Confirmed intent

Turn RO from “working ambiently” into “running a plan toward your date” — through an invitation, never a gate. RO proposes the goal pre-filled from everything she’s learned (you edit a nearly-done thing), then asks only what she can’t know: the date, whether it’s a real wall or an ambition (inferred when possible, asked warmly when not), and how hard to push (three modes with a live week preview; she recommends the sustainable one). The reveal: honest backward math with ranges landing on one doable number over a soft-edged four-phase road. Unrealistic goals get candor + levers at set-time, keepable eyes-open. Every input feeds the taste model; everything recalculates on change. Worked if users can say “what am I doing this week and why” unaided and half of active users set a goal within a week — while no-goal ambient mode stays legitimate.

Locked decisions

#DecisionChoice
1When to askInvited, never forced. Feed card is the main door; “not yet” dismisses gently, card returns quietly; always reachable. No goal = legitimate ambient mode.
2CollectionShe proposes, you edit — pre-filled from onboarding + all corrections. Editing teaches her (decision events).
3Plan revealHonest math + soft-edged timeline: funnel ranges (“good news — doable, here’s why”) → ONE this-week number (“I’ve already drafted 3”) → four-phase band (ramp/push/interviews/close), soft edges, only the deadline is a hard mark.
4Unrealistic goalCandid at set-time + levers, warmly (“I love the ambition — let’s be smart about it together”): widen / extend / keep-and-sprint-eyes-open. Never blocked, never blind.
5Deadline natureReal wall vs ambition — infer first, ask warmly only if unknown. Known (e.g. visa in profile) → confirm with care, never re-quiz. Wall → front-loaded pace + true-deadline reminders may cross quiet hours (only this). Ambition → steady pace, quiet hours absolute, date slides gracefully. Both answers framed as good answers; both get motivation.
6IntensitySteady / Push / All-in — game-feel, not game mechanics (no XP/streaks — banned). Each mode translated to real numbers + a live “your week with RO” preview; picking a mode personalizes her behavior. She recommends the sustainable choice and names what’s good about the ones she passes over. Switchable anytime, plan recalculates, no judgment.
7SuccessUser can answer “what am I doing this week and why” unaided; ≥50% of active users set a goal within a week of onboarding. NOT goal-set rate above all.
8Voice bar (feedback rule)Every line runs acknowledge → truth → forward-with-belief. Facts stay; delivery leaves the user feeling backed. Copy check: “would you feel backed and capable after reading this?” Applies to labels too (“we sprint, eyes open” not “at risk from day 0” user-facing).

Data & guardrails

Taste captureEvery edit to her proposal, the wall/ambition answer, mode choice + switches, lever picks = decision events feeding the taste model.
WellbeingNo streaks/XP/guilt. Falling behind on an ambition = zero-guilt, date slides. Only a real wall’s true cutoff may cross quiet hours, gently. She recommends rest when ahead.
Engine reusePlan/pace math, feasibility gate, W7 goal switching all exist in the app — this feature re-skins their moments, it does not rebuild them.
