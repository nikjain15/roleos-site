Settings / account / data controls — design decisions

Status: APPROVED (2026-07-05) · PRD shipped: roleos-app PR #60 (batched J12–J17) · prototype at /proto/settings/ · Unifies existing prefs + the taste/voice models into one surface.

Confirmed intent

Not a config page — the contract surface, where the product's promises become inspectable. Everything she knows: taste model + voice profile in plain English, evidence and confidence shown, correct/strike inline (the same ledger as the weekly review). Everything she does: every dial in one place — notifications, quiet hours, push intensity, overnight hunt, interviewer research, feedback depth, connections — with the one non-dial stated proudly: sending on your behalf is not a setting, it's never. Everything you've flagged: the 'not right?' history and what structurally changed because of each. And the exits: export-everything and delete-everything as real, honest buttons — leaving as honest as staying.

Locked decisions

Identity (keystone)The contract surface (human): knows / does / flags / exits — four zones, promises made inspectable.
The ledgerShared with weekly review; correct/strike anywhere, one underlying model.
The non-dial'Send on my behalf' shown as never-a-setting — the invariant displayed, not hidden.
ExitsExport + delete, one honest confirm, zero dark patterns; pause keeps everything without pings.
