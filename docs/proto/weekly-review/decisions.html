CORE · Weekly strategy review — design decisions

Status: IN FEEDBACK · Stage 1 confirmed 2026-07-05 (batched keystone round) · prototype at /proto/weekly-review/ · EXTENDS X7 (review page exists).

Confirmed intent

Ten minutes of stepping back that keep the weeks pointed at the goal. Good weeks: the honest numbers, what's working (do more of this — with evidence), and ONE focus for next week including rest-as-strategy. Rough weeks lead companion-first: acknowledge, the honest pattern, one fixable thing, belief — charts available, never leading. The learning ledger is the keystone: what she learned about you this week in plain English, each entry with evidence and confidence, confirmable / correctable / strikable on the spot — the self-learning model shown to and steered by its owner, at the moment she shows her work.

Locked decisions

The ledger (keystone)Taste-model updates in plain English, evidence + confidence per entry, confirm/fix/strike inline (human). Corrections = highest-weight signal. Also lives in Settings; steering moment is HERE.
RegistersGood-week = numbers+focus; rough-week = companion-first (voice formula), pattern-with-a-fix framing.
Focus disciplineONE next-week focus, never five; pace changes proposed, not imposed.
