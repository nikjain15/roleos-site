CORE · Referral & warm-intro finder — design decisions

Status: IN FEEDBACK · Stage 1 confirmed 2026-07-05 (batched keystone round) · prototype at /proto/referrals/ · Builds X6's approved PRD (sources A+D only — your data + public records; NO LinkedIn scraping).

Confirmed intent

Warm paths found, never spent for you. Sources are exactly the approved two: your own connected data (A) and public records (D) — provenance shown per path. Before drafting, one honest question: how well do you actually know this person? — the answer shapes the whole ask (friend ≠ acquaintance ≠ barely). Every ask is a per-person draft sent from YOUR accounts; RoleOS never contacts anyone, there is no batch mode, and semi-automated sending was explicitly rejected — your relationships are not hers to spend. No path found = said straight, with the cold-note alternative and a name-someone door.

Locked decisions

The gate (keystone)Per-person drafts, you send from your own accounts (human). Auto/semi-auto sending rejected on principle — the line doesn't move.
SourcesA + D only, provenance visible per path; the X6 ruling on LinkedIn (last, prefer never) holds.
Closeness shapingAsked once per person; drafts genuinely differ by relationship; honest asks over fake familiarity.
