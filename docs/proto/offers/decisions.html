Status: IN FEEDBACK · Stage 1 confirmed 2026-07-05 (batched keystone round) · prototype at /proto/offers/ · EXTENDS X5 (benchmarks, scenario modeling, counters exist).

Confirmed intent

The biggest decision of the journey, not faced alone. Single offer: celebrate first, then the honest benchmark with receipts (stated bands + comparables, estimates labeled) and levers each with honest odds. The counter is drafted — one ask, a repeatable reason, an easy yes — and sent by your hand, every round. Multi-offer: trajectory-aware comparison beyond money, and her lean given with receipts — citing what you taught her, linkably — then the wheel handed back warmly. Whichever you choose, she makes the close graceful and keeps other doors warm.

Locked decisions

DirectnessRecommends with receipts, call is yours (human): her lean cites linkable taste moments; never neutral-spreadsheet abandonment, never pushy.
LeversEach lever carries honest likelihood + basis; one-ask counters; multi-round drafted, every send yours.
BenchmarksStated vs estimated always labeled (confidence ladder); receipts tap-to-source.
