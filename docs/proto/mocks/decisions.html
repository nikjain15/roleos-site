CORE · Practice & mock interviews — design decisions

Status: APPROVED (2026-07-05) · PRD shipped: roleos-app PR #60 (batched J12–J17) · prototype at /proto/mocks/ · EXTENDS the coach (question prediction, story mapping, debriefs exist).

Confirmed intent

Ready is a feeling you earn. Three modes, the user's order: quick drills (recall), answer crafting (STAR built together, ends spoken-not-scripted), and the centerpiece — the adaptive mock where RO BECOMES the interviewer (real persona from the research brief, follow-ups that walk through doors your answers open). Story-bank gaps get fixed before drilling. Debriefs are gains-oriented: what landed, ONE thing to sharpen, re-mock just that, an honest readiness read that includes permission to rest. Voice mode is designed as a state — same flow out loud, same debrief plus what only audio can hear — shipping later per the X8 call (browser-native).

Locked decisions

ModesAll three (human combo): drills + answer crafting + adaptive role-play mock. Role-play is the centerpiece; nothing may end scripted-sounding.
The mockShe embodies the actual interviewer (brief-fed persona + lens); adaptive follow-ups; pause anytime; nothing graded until the debrief.
DebriefGains-first, ONE sharpening item, re-mock-the-weak-spot, readiness meter with honest framing (rest as strategy). No autonomy setting — coach mode by design.
Voice stateDesigned now, ships later: identical flow + audio-only feedback (pace, filler, presence). Zero switching cost by design.
