Explore & role discovery — design decisions

Status: IN FEEDBACK · Stage 1 confirmed 2026-07-04 · prototype at /proto/explore/ · becomes the formal PRD on APPROVED. Explore exists in the app (index browse + Ask + W1 fit badges + W6 anon persistence) — this unifies it into one conversational-browse surface.

Confirmed intent

Explore is RoleOS’s proving ground — where a skeptic tests “an agent runs your hunt” on real roles they choose. A conversational browse: RO greets first and her ask-bar leads, but her answers act directly on a scannable, filterable list — one surface, no forced typing. Cards carry honest essentials: pay labeled “their number” vs “my estimate,” posted-and-checked timestamps, one line of her judgment (“what this job really needs”). Fit is never faked: anonymous visitors get the truth — she can’t know fit until she knows them — as a warm 30-second invitation landing in onboarding’s Explore arrival. Closed roles are marked and redirected, never silently deleted. Signed-in users see real fit everywhere, and “why didn’t you show me this?” becomes trust: honest reasoning, their override, her learning. Empty results get a straight answer + the closest almost-fit labeled + an offer to keep watch. Success: Explore→onboarding starts and saves; gap moments resolve either direction without eroding trust.

Locked decisions

#DecisionChoice
1Primary userAnonymous evaluator first — Explore is the funnel’s “prove it” moment. Signed-in = same surface + fit + save.
2ShapeConversational browse (human chose 1+2 combined): RO speaks first, ask-bar leads, answers re-filter/re-sort the visible list with her one-line commentary; chips update to match; scrolling/filtering never requires typing.
3Anon fitHonest tease with a taste — objective read for everyone; fit column: “fit is about YOU — 30 seconds and I’ll be straight” → onboarding’s warm Explore arrival. No fake scores, ever.
4Card depthHonest essentials: company/role · pay labeled stated-vs-estimate · posted + checked-by-RO · one “what this job really needs” line · full read one tap away.
5The gap momentShe explains herself + acts — on-demand honest reasoning for roles she passed on; “want it anyway? teaches me”; both answers are decision events (override = high weight).
6FreshnessHonest timestamps + checked badge; closed roles marked, not hidden — with condolences + cousins (“this one closed — three close cousins are open”).
7Empty resultsStraight no + closest almost-fit labeled (“3 of 4”) + keep-watch offer (account bridge).
8SuccessCuriosity → onboarding: Explore→onboarding starts, share that save; gap-moment resolution either direction. No time-on-page worship (healthy-engagement family).
9VoiceWarm / plain / common-user everywhere, per the standing warm-copy rule.

Flags for the PRD

“May level down” warningsImplies detecting title inflation from posting language + comp signals. Plausible with the existing extraction + reasoning tier, but it’s a claim — the PRD will require calibrated wording (“reads like”) + an eval before shipping the label.
On-demand gap reasoning costOne reasoning call per “why not this?” tap. Worth it (trust moment); meter to agent_runs, rate-limit like other AI routes.
Checked-cadence exposure“Checked by RO Xd ago” shows our refresh rhythm publicly — ties to the role-refresh loop plan; PRD notes the dependency.
