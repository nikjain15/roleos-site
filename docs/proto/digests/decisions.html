Nudges & digests — design decisions

Status: IN FEEDBACK · Stage 1 confirmed 2026-07-05 (batched keystone round) · prototype at /proto/digests/ · Implements journey §10's locked interrupt economy; owns the rough-patch register deferred from J3.

Confirmed intent

She earns the interrupt. The daily digest is the feed's morning brief delivered — her two sentences, the finishable Today list, one pace line; same voice, no new information by design (a door, not a second product). Cadence is one setting (real-time / daily default / weekly / only-when-I-open); quiet hours absolute. The rough-patch register lives here and in the in-app brief: after real hard signals, cheer drops and companion leads — one gentle thing, everything else carried. Push is rare, earned, user-actionable only (~1/day, ~3/week cap); the never-list (streaks, guilt, ACT NOW) is banned in code; when you go quiet she goes quieter.

Locked decisions

Digest shape (keystone)Mirror of the feed brief (human): brief → Today → pace, one product two doors. Weekly-magazine rejected.
Rough-patch registerOwned here (deferred from J3): triggered by real signals only; companion-first copy per prototype; applies to in-app brief too.
Push economyJourney §10 rules verbatim: rare, earned, capped, quiet-hours-absolute, never-list banned in code.
