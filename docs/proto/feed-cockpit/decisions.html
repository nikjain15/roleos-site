Feed / cockpit — design decisions

Status: APPROVED (2026-07-04) · PRD shipped: roleos-app PR #43 (stacked on #39) · prototype at /proto/feed-cockpit/ · becomes the formal PRD on APPROVED. The feed exists in the app (slice 2 + slices 7–9) — this deepens the daily experience.

Confirmed intent

The feed is RoleOS’s home and its daily proof that an agent is genuinely running your hunt. Every open answers “what needs me” and “am I on pace” in ten seconds. RO’s short brief flows into a capped, finishable Today list (goal-aware; decisions-led with a quiet invitation when no goal), then a full honest activity log — every line expandable to her reasoning, routine batches one truthful line. Light judgments happen on the card (reasoning + diff one tap away; “open full view” always offered); heavy work opens its screen. Zones are user-arrangeable and RO proposes layout changes from observed behavior — never rearranging alone. Quiet days: honest calm + at most one real optional move from actual gap analysis; rest stays legitimate. Success = healthy engagement: unprompted return, Today completion, decision speed — explicitly not session length.

Locked decisions

#DecisionChoice
1What leadsCombined: RO’s 1–2 sentence morning brief flowing into the goal-aware Today list — capped ~3, always finishable, minutes labeled, completion celebrated (“you’re done for today — genuinely”).
2Her activityFull visible log — everything she did, timestamped, every line expandable to reasoning. Routine batches compress to one honest line so diligence never buries decisions.
3Inline depthLight judgments on the card (approve/send, skip, dismiss) with her why + diff preview one tap away; heavy work (studio, editing, mocks) opens full screen; “open full view” always offered.
4StructureThree base zones (Today+goal · needs judgment · activity log), fully personalizable: reorder / collapse / hide, saved per user.
5PersonalizationYou arrange + RO suggests from observed behavior (“you skip the log in the morning — collapse it before noon?”). She never rearranges on her own. Every arrangement choice = decision event.
6Quiet dayHonest calm + one REAL optional move drawn from actual gap analysis (e.g. missing interview story). Never manufactured busywork; rest is a first-class suggestion she genuinely endorses.
7No-goal feedSame home, decisions lead; goal invitation lives quietly in the flow. No punishment browse-mode; setting a goal sharpens the same screen.
8Heavy dayToday stays capped; overflow waits visibly-but-quietly (“3 more for tomorrow — on purpose; nothing time-sensitive, I checked”).
9SuccessHealthy engagement (human-agreed after candid pushback): unprompted daily return · Today completion rate · decision latency. Explicitly NOT session length/scroll depth.

Data & guardrails

Taste captureEvery inline judgment, skip-with-reason, layout choice, suggestion accept/decline = decision events.
Human gateInline “send” is still the user’s explicit click on a surfaced draft with reasoning + diff available — the send path stays /api/apply-shaped; nothing outbound without the click.
WellbeingCompletion is celebrated, absence is never punished; quiet days legitimize rest; no engagement bait anywhere.
