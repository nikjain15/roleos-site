Role workspace — design decisions

Status: APPROVED (2026-07-04) · PRD shipped: roleos-app PR #47 (stacked on #46) · prototype at /proto/role-workspace/ · becomes the formal PRD on APPROVED. The workspace exists (slice 5 + W4 compare/notes/bulk-dismiss) — this reshapes it into the decision room.

Confirmed intent

The role workspace is the decision room — where RO’s sourcing becomes your judgment, sized to your pace. A queue leads, never infinite: “five need your call this week,” each decided in about a minute, with a real finish line she celebrates and defends. Each role page puts her verdict first — the call, the honest why, gaps labeled bridgeable-or-stretch — with pursue/pass right beneath, and the posting + notes one optional tap below. Compare is a tie-breaker with a dense table’s soul: she offers it at genuine ties, you can summon it for any 2–3, her lean cites what you’ve taught her. Passing is free and undoable; explaining is warmly encouraged and immediately rewarded with a visible re-weighting. Pursuing visibly starts her work — the tailoring chain, every step human-gated — and the queue advances. The full list stays browsable beneath, pruned by her, never by you. Worked if: queues get emptied, decisions come fast, and a healthy share of passes teach her why.

Locked decisions

#DecisionChoice
1Its jobA decision queue to empty, sized by the plan’s pace (“decide on these 5 this week”); the full saved list beneath, kept fresh + pruned by RO. Backlogs never guilt (“you still only need to decide on five”).
2Role pageHer verdict leads + actions right beneath (human chose B1+B3): call → honest why → gaps (bridgeable/stretch) → pursue/pass buttons → posting + notes as optional taps. Decide and act on one screen.
3CompareTie-breaker entry, table presentation (human chose C1+C2): offered when she spots a genuine tie or when you select 2–3; renders as the dense differences-only table; her lean cites your taught preferences and hands the call back.
4PassingOne tap + undo, why warmly encouraged (never required) — chips + free-text (“say it your way”), with an immediate visible payoff (“that just re-weighted how I rank for you”). Human: actively encourage typing the why — it feeds personalization.
5Pursue semanticsPursue visibly starts her work: resume tailoring now → cover letter after your OK → ready-to-send bundle (you press send). The chain is shown; queue advances.
6Finish lineQueue completion is celebrated + defended (“you’ve earned the evening”); next batch has a named day. No infinite queue, no “one more.”
7SuccessQueue completion rate · decision latency · share of passes with reasons. Healthy-engagement family.
8VoiceWarm-copy bar everywhere (standing rule); recommendations praise the passed-over option; labels point forward.

Data & guardrails

Taste capturePursue/pass, every reason (chip or free text — free text = highest value), compare picks, undo = decision events; pass-reasons re-weight ranking visibly.
Human gatePursue starts drafting only — nothing outbound; the send stays in Apply/Send with the user’s click.
HonestyGaps always shown with bridgeable/stretch labels; her lean always cites its basis; pay provenance labels carried from Explore.
