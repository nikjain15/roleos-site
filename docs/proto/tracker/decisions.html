Tracker — design decisions

Status: APPROVED (2026-07-05) · PRD shipped: roleos-app PR #50 (stacked on #49) · prototype at /proto/tracker/. Tracker exists (slice 3 + W5 depth) — this reshapes the daily experience of it.

Confirmed intent (from the four answers + pre-authorized build)

The tracker is the funnel of record that keeps itself true — a kanban-shaped board whose cards move themselves (human’s combination: kanban view + self-maintaining record). Stages advance from real events — sends, parsed replies, interview dates, your one-tap reports; dragging a card is a correction, welcomed and learned from, never homework. Her pipeline read leads: one honest paragraph of what’s moving, what’s quiet-but-normal, and whose move each thing is (yours / hers / theirs — badged on every card). Aging is honest and contextual (“14 days — normal for their size” vs “24 — past typical; your nudge is drafted”), with gentle closure proposed around 30 days (“no reply isn’t a no on your worth”) and a keep-hoping override that’s excluded from pace math. Rejections get the full candid-never-cold treatment — information not verdict, a quiet collapsed closed section, and pattern-learning said out loud (“two passes at final round — I’m shifting your mock focus”). Every outcome feeds the funnel calibration. Worked if: the answer to “where does everything stand?” takes one glance, nobody drags cards as chores, and closures/rejections leave the user steadied, not stung.

Locked decisions

#DecisionChoice
1NatureKanban + self-maintaining (human combo): board VIEW, cards advance from events; drag = correction that teaches (decision event) and never a maintenance duty. Without Gmail connected, one-tap confirms replace parsing — asked warmly, never nagged.
2What leadsHer pipeline read — one checkable paragraph; board below with whose-move badges (yours/hers/theirs) on every card.
3AgingHonest aging + gentle closure: contextual quiet-time norms per company; nudge drafted at “past typical”; ~30d closure proposal with keep-hoping override (excluded from pace math, still watched).
4RejectionsCandid-never-cold + learned-from: voice-formula delivery; collapsed closed section (no wall of shame); pattern adjustments said out loud; outcomes feed funnel calibration (X4 wiring).
5SuccessOne-glance clarity; zero chore-drags (drags only as corrections); post-rejection/closure sentiment steadied. Healthy-engagement family.
6VoiceWarm-copy bar; “no reply isn’t a no on your worth” register throughout; whose-move badges keep responsibility honest.

Data & guardrails

Self-maintenance sources/api/apply sends · Gate-2 inbox parsing (where connected) · calendar · one-tap user reports. Unverifiable states stay honest (“you told me” provenance).
Taste/funnel captureStage corrections, keep-hoping choices, nudge sends, closure accepts = decision events; every outcome updates personal funnel rates (dim 14 / X4).
Human gateNudges and follow-ups are drafted, you-send, via the feed. The tracker never sends anything.
