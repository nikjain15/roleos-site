Apply / Send — design decisions

Status: IN FEEDBACK · Stage 1 confirmed 2026-07-04 · prototype at /proto/apply-send/ · becomes the formal PRD on APPROVED. Apply exists (slice 4 + W2 letters + X3 quality score) — this deepens the ceremony around the product’s one outward gate.

Confirmed intent

Apply/Send is the sacred gate — the only place work leaves the building, and it leaves with your hand on it. One review screen: her one-line summary per piece, quality verdict on top as an advisor (one-tap fixes that show their work and re-score, never a blocked send), and every piece fully editable in place — edits welcomed as voice lessons and quietly re-scored. The handoff is owned proudly: the last click happens on the company’s site on purpose, made two-minutes-easy with per-field copy blocks. The truth gate holds at the sharpest point — she won’t invent answers, offers honest reframes, invites you to teach her. After the send: real celebration, honest odds, her visible next move (watching + day-10 follow-up drafted). Tracker advances itself. Worked if: sends happen with genuine review, fixes get taken, post-send anxiety stays low.

Locked decisions

#DecisionChoice
1Review shapeOne screen, everything expandable AND fully editable in place (human chose 1 + 2’s editability): summaries → open → edit any piece; edits get a warm voice-lesson response and quiet re-score. No forced stepper; the gate is the click, made genuinely informed.
2Quality scoreAdvisory + one-tap fixes, never blocks. Fixes show the exact new line (highlighted, editable) and re-score visibly. Weak bundles (61) get honest framing + two fixes; “I never hold the door shut.”
3The handoffOwned proudly — “the last click happens on their site, on purpose: nothing leaves without your hand.” Per-field copy blocks in their form’s order; tailored PDF download; email variant opens a filled Gmail draft. Unfinished handoffs wait without guilt.
4Truth gate momentWhen a question asks what the profile doesn’t support: she refuses to invent, offers an honest reframe that still lands, and invites the user to teach her the missing story. Surfaced BEFORE the send, in the review.
5Post-sendCelebrate + honest odds + her watch: the win first, then “~1 in 4 gets a reply, usually 2 weeks” (personalized once real rates accrue), then what’s hers to carry (reply watch, day-10 follow-up drafted). Tracker advances automatically.
6SuccessGenuine-review signals (expand/edit rates healthy, not zero) · fix take-rate · post-send return-without-anxious-refresh. Healthy-engagement family.
7VoiceWarm-copy bar throughout; celebration genuine; odds honest; never guilt on unfinished sends.

Data & guardrails

Human gateUnchanged and celebrated: /api/apply-shaped path only; the final action always happens on the user’s explicit click (their site or their mail app). The design makes the gate a feature, not a fallback.
Taste captureEvery edit (voice lessons — high weight), fix accept/decline, truth-gate choices, send/abandon = decision events. Edits feed the resume/letter voice model.
Truth gateEnforced, not optional — no invented claims survive to the bundle; reframes trace to master_profile.
