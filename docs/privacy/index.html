Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

This policy explains how RoleOS ("we", "us") handles your information when you use roleos.fyi and the RoleOS service. We keep this simple on purpose.

What we collect

We collect the information you give us — like your email when you join the waitlist or create an account — and the content you choose to add while using the product. We also collect basic technical data (such as device and usage information) needed to run and secure the service.

How we use it

We use your information to provide and improve the service, keep your account secure, and communicate with you. We only take actions on your behalf that you approve. We do not sell your information, and we do not use your personal content to train AI models.

Sharing

We share information only with the service providers that help us operate RoleOS, and only as needed to run the service. We may also share information where required by law or to protect the safety and security of our users and the service.

Security & retention

We use reasonable measures to protect your information and keep it only as long as needed to provide the service. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to keep your data safe.

Your choices

You can access, update, or delete your information, and unsubscribe from emails at any time. To make a request, just email us.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we'll update the date above and let you know.

Contact

Questions about your data? Email ro@roleos.fyi.
