Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how RoleOS ("RoleOS", "we", "us", or "our") — a product operated by Nik Jain — collects, uses, shares, and protects information about you when you visit roleos.fyi, join our waitlist, or use the RoleOS application (together, the "Service").

RoleOS is an AI agent that helps you run a senior job search end-to-end. We take your data seriously: your personal job-search materials are yours, we never take an outward action (like sending an email or submitting an application) without your approval, and we do not sell your data.

1. Information we collect

Information you give us

  • Waitlist & contact details — your email address when you join the waitlist or contact us.
  • Account information — when you create a RoleOS account, your email and authentication details. We support sign-in by magic link and by Google OAuth; if you use Google, we receive your name, email, and profile basics from Google (we do not receive your Google password).
  • Job-search content — materials you provide or create in the product, such as your CV/résumé, work history, target roles and companies, application notes, preferences, and drafts that RO prepares with you.
  • Communications — messages you send us, and content of applications or outreach you approve RO to send on your behalf.

Information we collect automatically

  • Usage & device data — basic technical information such as IP address, browser and device type, pages viewed, and timestamps, used to operate and secure the Service.
  • Cookies & local storage — we use essential cookies and browser storage to keep you signed in and remember your preferences. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

Information from public sources

To power the public Index and your searches, RoleOS reads publicly available job postings from company careers pages and applicant-tracking systems (such as Greenhouse, Lever, and Ashby). This is public information about roles, not personal information about you.

2. How we use your information

  • To provide, operate, and improve the Service — including finding relevant roles, scoring your fit, drafting application materials, and tracking your conversations.
  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To take actions you explicitly approve, such as submitting an application or sending outreach. RO never sends anything outward without your approval.
  • To communicate with you about the waitlist, product access, and important Service updates.
  • To detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, security incidents, and technical issues.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use your personal job-search content to train AI models.

3. AI processing

RoleOS uses third-party large language models — primarily Anthropic's Claude — to read job descriptions, assess fit, and draft content. To do this, relevant content (for example, a job description and the parts of your CV needed for a task) is sent to our AI provider's API to generate a response.

Our AI provider processes this content to return outputs to us and, per its API terms, does not use API inputs or outputs to train its models. AI-generated drafts are suggestions for your review — you decide what to keep, edit, or send.

4. How we share your information

We share information only as described here:

  • Service providers — vendors who process data on our behalf to run the Service (see the list below), under contracts that require them to protect your data.
  • At your direction — when you approve RO to submit an application or send outreach, the relevant content is delivered to the recipient or platform you chose.
  • Legal & safety — when required by law, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of RoleOS, our users, or the public.
  • Business transfers — if RoleOS is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may transfer as part of that transaction; we will notify you of any change in control.

6. Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. If you ask us to delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within a reasonable period, except where we must retain it to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce our agreements. Waitlist emails are kept until you unsubscribe or ask us to remove them.

7. Security

We use technical and organizational measures — including encryption in transit, access controls, and reputable infrastructure providers — to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security, but we work to protect your data and to notify you of incidents where required by law.

8. Your rights and choices

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Delete your information.
  • Export a copy of your information (data portability).
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.
  • Unsubscribe from emails at any time using the link in any message, or by contacting us.

To exercise any of these, email us at ro@roleos.fyi. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law and may need to verify your identity first.

9. U.S. state privacy rights

If you are a resident of California or another U.S. state with a comprehensive privacy law, you have the rights to know, access, correct, delete, and obtain a portable copy of your personal information, and to not be discriminated against for exercising these rights.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising. We do not knowingly process sensitive personal information for purposes that would require an opt-out. To exercise your rights, contact ro@roleos.fyi.

10. International users

RoleOS is operated with infrastructure that may process and store data in the United States and other countries. If you access the Service from outside the United States, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in countries whose data-protection laws may differ from those in your jurisdiction. Where required (for example, for users in the EEA or UK), we rely on appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses for international transfers, and you retain the rights described above.

11. Children's privacy

RoleOS is intended for working professionals and is not directed to anyone under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above and, for material changes, provide additional notice (such as by email or an in-product notice). Your continued use of the Service after an update means you accept the revised policy.
