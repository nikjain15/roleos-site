Questions

Things people ask before signing up.

Is the chat actually grounded, or is it making things up?

RO only answers from the 458 real JDs it has read. If something isn't in the data, RO will say so.

Is it free?

Early users get free access during beta. Pricing will be announced before your free period ends. No card to join the waitlist.

How does RoleOS know which companies to track?

For the public Index, we curated 79 AI-native companies. For your own version, you give it a list — any company with a Greenhouse, Lever, or Ashby careers page works.

Does RO apply for me?

Yes. RO drafts the cover letter, fills the form, and submits — for any company with a Greenhouse, Lever, or Ashby careers page. You set the rules: auto-apply where your fit score is high, require approval below it. You see every application before it sends.

What about my data?

The public Index here is public job postings — nothing personal. Your CV, application notes, and search history stay on your machine.

When does v1 open?

We're opening founding-user access in waves over the coming weeks. Drop your email and we'll be in touch when your wave is up.

Who is this for?

Senior PMs and PM-adjacent ICs — TPM, BizOps, Strategy — targeting AI-native companies. We're staying narrow on purpose. Once we serve this group well, we'll expand.

Will you spam me?

No. One email when v1 opens. Maybe one more if there's something genuinely worth saying. Unsubscribe with one click.
