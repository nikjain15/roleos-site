Why this exists

Finding the right role is a research problem.
You've been treating it like a sales one.

Every JD is a 1,200-word requirements doc. Every company is a strategy puzzle. AI PMs deserve tooling that matches the rigor of the work itself — not another tracker.

What RoleOS does

Three things. End to end.

Scope every role that matters

Crawls ATS portals (Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby) across your target companies. Filters to the roles you'd actually take.

Read every JD like a PM

Claude extracts archetype, seniority, must-haves with verbatim quotes, salary, location, visa — into a strict schema. No skimming. No hallucination.

Track the funnel, end to end

From scoped to offer. See where you stand, what's stalled, where your time goes. The CRM your job search deserved.
