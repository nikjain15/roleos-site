## What the role involves

**Surface:** Enterprise customer deployments of Scale AI platform — embedded in customer accounts, driving from contract to production

**Business model:** B2B enterprise AI platform

**Team size:** unclear

**Cross-functional partners:** Forward Deployed Engineers (FDEs), Core platform teams, VP/C-level customer stakeholders, Customer engineering teams, Scale leadership

**Primary metrics (inferred):** Deployments reaching production; Customer value realization and expansion; Quality and specificity of product signal fed back to platform team
**Core responsibilities:**

- Own product outcomes for our highest impact enterprise accounts. You are the accountable PM for product delivery and customer value.
- Build trusted advisory relationships with VP/C-level buyers and senior technical leads.
- Drive deployments from contract to production.
- Translate real customer workflows, friction, and priorities into high-signal product input for core platform teams.
- Distinguish where the product is the constraint from where execution, integration, or change management is.
- Partner closely with FDEs so the account runs as a coordinated team, not a set of parallel workstreams.
- Identify and develop expansion opportunities grounded in demonstrated customer value
- Represent customer reality to platform and leadership with enough credibility that it changes decisions.
