## What the role involves

**Surface:** Post-sale customer lifecycle management for a portfolio of commercial LangSmith customers

**Business model:** B2B SaaS, consumption/usage-based pricing, developer-first platform

**Team size:** unclear

**Cross-functional partners:** Sales, GTM Engineering, RevOps, Product, Engineering

**Primary metrics (inferred):** Gross Renewal Rate; Net Revenue Retention; Quarterly and annual renewal targets; Customer retention rates
**Core responsibilities:**

- Manage the entire customer lifecycle post signature for a portfolio of commercial customers, from adoption to contract execution, ensuring high retention rates.
- Develop and maintain a rolling forecast of your portfolio and consistently meet or exceed quarterly and annual renewal and net retention targets.
- Proactively identify and mitigate churn risk by understanding customer health, monitoring product usage, and addressing commercial obstacles.
- Uncover and execute on expansion opportunities by understanding customer needs, aligning them with new features and products, and articulating the incremental value.
- Build and nurture lasting relationships with key stakeholders, from technical users to economic buyers.
- Act as the voice of the customer, synthesizing feedback on value, product gaps, and business outcomes to inform the product roadmap.
- Partner closely with Sales, GTM Engineering, and RevOps teams to present a unified post-sale experience.
- Lead commercial negotiations, structure renewal contracts, and manage quoting and procurement processes.
