## What the role involves

**Surface:** AI-powered outreach and guidance motion for self-serve customers (inbound chat, voice, email, onboarding, cross-sell, qualification, handoff to sales)

**Business model:** B2B and B2B2C, product-led growth for self-serve (startups and SMBs)

**Team size:** unclear

**Cross-functional partners:** Engineering, AI/ML, Design, Data Science, Marketing, Sales, Support, Vendor support team

**Primary metrics (inferred):** Inbound resolution rates; Answer quality; Conversion through activation and expansion; Pipeline generated through AI-qualified leads; Self-serve revenue
**Core responsibilities:**

- Define the AI specialist: what it knows, how it engages, where it draws the line, and when it hands off. Own the product vision, strategy, and roadmap.
- Launch and scale the inbound motion across chat, voice, and email for prospects and new users seeking help with products, pricing, and getting started.
- Build the triage and qualification layer to assess users, understand opportunity size, and route accordingly.
- Define escalation paths to the vendor support team and sales team.
- Scale past the first interaction into onboarding and cross-sell.
- Leverage Stripe's existing product recommendation, merchant context, onboarding systems, and outreach platform.
- Own the revenue this motion produces and define success metrics.
- Partner with engineering, AI/ML, design, data science, marketing, sales, and support.
