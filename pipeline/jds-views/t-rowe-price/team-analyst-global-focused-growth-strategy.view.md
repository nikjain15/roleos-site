**Surface:** Global Focused Growth Strategy — a complex, diversified global equity portfolio managed by Portfolio Manager Dave Eiswert

**Business model:** active asset management, B2B and institutional

**Team size:** unclear

**Cross-functional partners:** Portfolio Manager (Dave Eiswert), Senior analysts, Public-company executives (CEOs/CFOs), External experts

**Primary metrics (inferred):** Portfolio performance vs. benchmark; Quality of investment recommendations / ex-ante vs. ex-post accuracy; Breadth and depth of research coverage
**Core responsibilities:**

- Adopt, execute, and continuously improve the Global Focused Growth investment framework (historical analysis, insight development, and forward modeling).
- Lead scoped company/industry research projects and thematic workstreams aligned to portfolio needs.
- Build and maintain three-statement and scenario models; synthesize market and alternative data into forecasts.
- Partner with the Portfolio Manager and senior analysts to prepare briefs, update views, and translate research into portfolio actions.
- Engage CEOs/CFOs and other external experts through meetings, calls, and road visits to test and refine theses.
- Extremely detail oriented documentation, compliance discipline, and data hygiene; contribute to reusable templates and best practices.
- Track ex-ante assumptions versus ex-post outcomes and participate in performance attribution reviews.
- Conduct primary diligence (filings, transcripts, channel checks, site visits) to deepen company and industry understanding.
- Develop investment recommendations over time and accept outcome accountability as responsibilities expand if appropriate.
- Design and apply AI-driven workflows (prompt libraries, retrieval, automation) to improve research speed and quality.
