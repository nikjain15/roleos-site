**Primary metrics (inferred):** OKR alignment and delivery of GSS strategic initiatives; Monthly KPI and quarterly Board reporting accuracy; Risk posture improvement metrics; Program delivery timeliness and control framework rollout progress
**Core responsibilities:**

- Lead program delivery for GSS's most complex initiatives including third-party risk, compliance and audit readiness, AI governance, and data-driven cyber risk and control framework
- Set and continuously refine the program rhythm: planning cycles, status reporting, OKR alignment, and decision logs
- Apply AI-enabled GSS tooling to reduce manual toil and improve program reporting
- Identify automation and AI opportunities in program management and operationalise them
- Run risk analysis, contingency planning, and trade-off conversations with senior stakeholders
- Maintain authoritative status materials for GSS leadership, monthly KPI updates, and quarterly Board contributions
- Act as a player-coach to other program managers across GSS and GTS
- Flex into other GSS functions where strategic initiatives need senior program leadership
