## What the role involves

**Surface:** User Trust — identity verification, authentication, device risk, behavioral signals, account security for Betterment's retail investing and cash products; tooling for Customer Experience and Operations teams serving 1M+ customers

**Business model:** B2C fintech (retail investing, savings, cash) and B2B2C (401k for SMBs)

**Team size:** unclear

**Cross-functional partners:** Engineering, Operations, Security, Compliance, Legal, Customer Experience, Analytics, Design, Other product teams

**Primary metrics (inferred):** Financial risk reduction; Login success rates; Signup and funding conversion rates; Customer experience quality alongside security controls
**Core responsibilities:**

- Own the product roadmap across identity verification, authentication, device risk, behavioral signals, and account security
- Identify and prioritize opportunities to increase security and integrity while minimizing friction in signup, funding, withdrawals, login, and sensitive profile changes
- Partner with Operations, Security Engineering, Customer Experience, and Compliance to design, implement, and iterate on KYC/KYB, 2FA, risk scoring, transaction monitoring, and case management controls
- Work closely with engineering to define solutions and tradeoffs
- Lead cross-functional delivery from problem definition through discovery, specs, launch, and iteration
- Run experiments and tests to calibrate trust controls
- Define and track success metrics that balance platform integrity with customer experience
- Build deep insight into customers and internal operators through research, risk and incident reviews, CX shadowing, and market and competitive analysis
