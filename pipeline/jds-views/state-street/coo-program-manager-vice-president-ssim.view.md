## What the role involves

**Surface:** Investment Management COO – Strategic Program Management across Product, Platform, and Services pillars

**Business model:** B2B institutional investment management and asset servicing

**Team size:** unclear

**Cross-functional partners:** IM COO Product pillar, IM COO Platform pillar, IM COO Services pillar, IM CAO Strategic Program Management, Technology teams, Business stakeholders, SMEs, Global teams

**Primary metrics (inferred):** Achievement of IM Objective Key Results (OKRs); On-time and on-scope delivery of strategic initiatives; Program budget and resource tracking accuracy
**Core responsibilities:**

- Be the primary face-off with the COO Investment Management Product, Platform and Services pillars and the IM CAO Strategic Program Management organization to deliver Strategic initiatives and drive to achieving IM Objective Key Results (OKRs).
- Understand/document requirements including the products being used in IM organizations to determine how they can be leveraged or eliminated.
- Drive the joint and internal governance with all stakeholders. This includes the development and maintenance of project plans, status reports, issue and action logs and scope and requirements documents.
- Drive closure on all aspects of the program with support from the functional teams and SME's, including: contracts and commercials, scope and solution, project sequencing and planning, development, testing, conversion and post conversion activities.
- Manage subject matter experts and project managers supporting workstreams to deliver the end-to-end solution.
- Lead and support governance meetings - set meeting agendas and document meeting minutes for meetings to discuss progress and address issues to achieve milestones and objectives for the execution of the plans
- Track resources, hours and hiring for resources, and rolling up globally with inputs from Global teams. Compile resource plans and program budgets.
- Actively communicate the status of work with other team members to ensure that everyone involved is fully informed.
