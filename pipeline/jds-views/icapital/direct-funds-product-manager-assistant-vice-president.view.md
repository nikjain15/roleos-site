## What the role involves

**Surface:** Post-launch fund servicing, lifecycle management, and governance for Luxembourg-domiciled AIFs (primarily Evergreen private markets funds) across Europe

**Business model:** B2B — fund servicing and product management platform for Asset and Wealth management clients

**Team size:** unclear

**Cross-functional partners:** Legal, Compliance, Tax, Client Success, Administrators, Fund Sponsors, Auditors, Corporate Secretary, Internal and External Distribution Channels

**Primary metrics (inferred):** Timely completion of fund servicing activities; Accuracy of due diligence and RFP responses; Regulatory compliance and approval timelines for new share classes/amendments
**Core responsibilities:**

- Serve as the primary point of contact for post-launch product management of Lux funds, including Evergreen private markets funds.
- Coordinate the launch of new share classes as well as oversee any other amendments to offering materials, including arranging for any associated regulatory approvals and managing investor consent processes, as applicable.
- Coordinate the production and compliance sign-off of key fund level marketing materials and ensure the timely dissemination of information to all relevant stakeholders
- Respond to due diligence (DD) questionnaires on funds serviced and/or managed by iCapital and oversee fund level RFPs, coordinating across functions for a timely delivery of accurate responses.
- Handle AML/KYC with key service providers pertaining to both upstream and downstream processes relating to the funds serviced/managed by iCapital
- Liaise with the International Tax team to respond to jurisdiction-specific tax queries and arrange for appropriate registrations in offshore jurisdictions.
- Handle ad hoc client requests relating to fund servicing, life cycle management and fund governance.
- Support the administration and governance of recurring fund servicing activities, ensuring timely and accurate completion.
- Collaborate with internal teams (Legal, Compliance, Tax, Client Success) and external providers (Administrators, Fund Sponsors, auditors) to deliver seamless fund servicing.
