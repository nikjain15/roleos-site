**Surface:** Revenue Control team within Corporate Finance; primarily Technology Solutions product line revenue

**Business model:** B2B fintech / alternative investment platform (technology solutions and managed services)

**Team size:** unclear

**Cross-functional partners:** Client relationship managers, Finance leadership, External client contacts, Business unit and product line leads

**Core responsibilities:**

- Spearhead month-end close processes, preparing revenue calculations and accruals
- Calculate and consolidate P&L and balance sheet data with accurate journal entries

- Support implementation of new order-to-cash billing and receivable software
- Develop insightful reporting and analyses for management and investors
- Identify process gaps and implement robust internal controls
- Leverage automation tools and data-driven workflows to improve efficiency
