## What the role involves

**Surface:** Quote-to-close process and commercial governance for RunPod's enterprise sales motion

**Business model:** B2B SaaS/IaaS, usage-based pricing, enterprise infrastructure

**Team size:** unclear

**Cross-functional partners:** Sales, Finance, Legal, Customer Success, Revenue Operations

**Primary metrics (inferred):** Deal velocity (SLA adherence); Pricing governance and discount compliance; Forecast and bookings accuracy; Contract repository completeness and CRM alignment
**Core responsibilities:**

- Manage the review and approval process for non standard enterprise deals
- Support custom pricing structures, savings plans, multi year agreements, and volume discounts
- Establish and maintain Deal Desk SLAs with the Sales organization
- Own CPQ implementation and administration in partnership with Revenue Operations and Finance
- Maintain pricing rules, discount matrices, approval workflows, and product catalogs
- Maintain pricing and discount governance standards
- Partner with Finance on bookings definitions and revenue recognition requirements
- Partner with Legal on contract review workflows and approval paths
- Manage executed contract repositories and ensure CRM alignment
- Build operational systems supporting renewals, amendments, and expansion workflows
- Ensure deal structures and contract values are accurately reflected in CRM systems
- Leverage AI tools for contract review, workflow automation, pricing analysis, and approval routing
