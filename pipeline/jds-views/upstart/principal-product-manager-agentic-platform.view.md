**Surface:** Internal Agentic Platform — infrastructure enabling autonomous AI agents across Onboarding, Servicing, Fraud, and Operations; includes tools for agent configuration, evaluation, orchestration, and integration

**Business model:** internal platform / developer tooling (B2B2E); Upstart is a fintech AI lending marketplace

**Team size:** unclear

**Cross-functional partners:** Engineering, Research, Legal, Compliance, Operations, Senior stakeholders

**Primary metrics (inferred):** Platform adoption across internal teams; Agent reliability, compliance, and production-readiness; Reduction in cost and latency through model lifecycle optimization; Speed and safety of agent deployment by non-technical users
**Core responsibilities:**

- Set and drive the long-term vision and multi-year strategy for Upstart's Agentic Platform
- Own and evolve the platform roadmap, making high-leverage prioritization decisions
- Lead development of product experiences enabling non-technical users to build, test, and deploy agents without writing code
- Establish evaluation, simulation, and safety frameworks for agents in a regulated environment
- Define architectural strategy for agentic systems including orchestration, tool usage, memory, RAG, and state management
- Drive company-wide adoption by partnering with senior stakeholders
- Make strategic decisions on model lifecycle management (frontier vs. fine-tuned/distilled)
- Define and standardize human-in-the-loop patterns
- Mentor other PMs and influence best practices
