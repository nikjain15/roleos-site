**Business model:** B2B SaaS, post-Series A

**Team size:** unclear

**Cross-functional partners:** Sales, Marketing, Customer Success, Finance, Product, Legal, Revenue Leadership

**Core responsibilities:**

- Influence strategic decisions by presenting data-backed recommendations on pricing, packaging, and contract structures
