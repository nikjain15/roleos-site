**Surface:** Referrals & Activations growth programs for Factor US brand

**Business model:** Direct-to-Consumer subscription meal kits

**Team size:** unclear

**Cross-functional partners:** Global Channel Owners, Data Analytics, Product Marketing, Senior Manager of Growth Strategy, Associate Director of Growth, Factor US, Local Product Marketing Manager, Cross-market Channel Managers

**Primary metrics (inferred):** Customer lifetime value (LTV); Total Value Generated from campaigns; Referral and activation program performance vs. targets; Customer acquisition efficiency
**Core responsibilities:**

- Steer RAF performance & strategy: Analyze and distill complex business insights to provide leadership with a comprehensive understanding of performance relative to targets, including a clear diagnosis of the underlying causes for any deviations.
- Optimize targeting for value generation: Deeply analyze campaign performance to recommend and implement optimizations to our targeting criteria, ensuring campaigns maximize the Total Value Generated rather than simply volume.

- Enhance product messaging efficiency: Collaborate with the local Product Marketing Manager to integrate the best product-level messages into campaigns, increasing messaging efficiency and ensuring we are always targeting the right customer with the right offer.
- Facilitate growth analysis and projects on Activations: Facilitate the execution of growth initiatives by working alongside the Senior Manager of Growth Strategy.
