## What the role involves

**Surface:** Product Portfolio Management team within HelloTech IT & Tech organization; processes including IT Capitalization, delivery reporting, R&D Tax Credit applications

**Business model:** subscription-based direct-to-consumer

**Team size:** 1000+ in HelloTech globally

**Cross-functional partners:** Tribe Leads, Engineering, Product Managers, Legal, Finance, Senior Leadership

**Primary metrics (inferred):** Budget variance tracking (spend vs. plan); Headcount and contractor ratio visibility; Compliance accuracy for IT Capitalization and R&D Tax Credit; Delivery reporting coverage and quality
**Core responsibilities:**

- Lead the improvement and automation of workflows such as IT capitalization, R&D tax credit applications, and delivery reporting, moving from manual individual inputs to an automated, tool-driven, AI-supported process.
- Serve as the primary point of contact for process audits, ensuring compliance and accurate documentation.
- Develop and maintain automated workflows integrating JIRA, GitHub, and Workday to capture technical work metadata for financial reporting in our HelloTech organization.
- Manage strategic projects to improve efficiency within HelloTech, such as improved Contractor Onboarding or the application of GenAI in reporting.
- Partner with Tribe Leads, Engineering, and Product Managers to gain insights and maintain and improve our reporting landscape for deliveries and key metrics.
- Support with the preparation of (Senior) Leadership meetings and Demo Sessions.
- Support tech leaders in tracking spend vs. plan, flagging variances, and maintaining visibility into headcount, open roles, and contractor ratios.
- Support quarterly and annual planning rhythms — OKR cycles, QBRs, AOP inputs — ensuring leadership has what they need, when they need it.
