# Director, Strategy & Operations - Service Delivery — Whatnot

## 🚀 Join the Future of Commerce with Whatnot!
Whatnot is the largest livestream shopping platform in North America and Europe to buy, sell, and discover the things you love. Whether it's trading cards, fashion, electronics, or live plants, our sellers are building real businesses across hundreds of categories. We're building live commerce at a scale that's never been done in the West, and there's no playbook to copy. The people here are shaping how an entirely new industry develops.

As a remote co-located team, we're inspired by our values and anchored in hubs across the US, UK, Ireland, Poland, Germany, and Australia. We move fast, stay close to our users, and focus on the work that drives the most impact.

We're one of the fastest growing marketplaces and were recently named the #1 Best Startup Employer in America by Forbes. Check out the latest Whatnot updates on our news and engineering blogs and join us as we enable anyone to turn their passion into a business and bring people together through commerce.

## 💻 Role
- Develop and execute commerce and operations strategies that support Whatnot’s growth across post-purchase operations, with a focus on scaling our Vendor footprint for customer experience and Trust & Risk–related issues

- Apply strong analytical rigor to operational and commerce data, surfacing insights that inform initiatives to improve quality, speed, and scalability across the Operations team

- Lead and set the strategy for Whatnot’s Operations vendor framework, deploying managers and specialists to drive outcomes, set priorities, guide execution, and ensure high-quality delivery for Whatnot’s users

- Define and track vendor KPIs, monitor quality and SLAs, and drive continuous performance improvement across partner sites

- Lead strategic sourcing efforts to expand vendor coverage across new capabilities or geographies in support of business growth and continuity

- Build scalable systems and processes that allow Customer Experience and Trust & Risk support teams to expand quickly while maintaining high-quality support for Whatnot’s users

- Partner cross-functionally with Product, Engineering, and other stakeholders to translate insights from user interactions into initiatives that support scale and improved outcomes

Team members in this role are required to be within commuting distance of our New York, San Francisco, Phoenix, or Los Angeles hubs.

## 👋 You
People who do well at Whatnot tend to be comfortable figuring things out as they go, biased toward action, and genuinely curious about what they're building. They care more about outcomes than credit and stay close to the product and the people using it.

As our next Strategy & Operations Director, you bring deep experience leading complex operations, driving cross-functional initiatives, and translating data into meaningful business outcomes in fast-paced, ambiguous environments.

You should have 12+ years of experience across strategy, operations, or general management roles (e.g., consulting, operations, finance, GM), plus:

- Proven experience operating in high-growth scaling environments, with a strong track record of driving operational improvement and building and leading high performing teams

- Strong strategic and analytical judgment, with the ability to synthesize data, metrics, and qualitative insights into clear recommendations and decisions

- Experience leading large, cross-functional initiatives and managing complex, multi-threaded projects end to end

- Proven ability to work with external partners or vendors in scaled, global operating models

- Exceptional leadership skills, with experience setting direction, guiding execution, and driving accountability across teams

- Ability to manage shifting priorities while maintaining structure, focus, and operational rigor

- Proficiency in data analysis and reporting tools; experience with SQL and analytics platforms (e.g., Looker, Sigma, Hex)
