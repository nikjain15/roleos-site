# Technical Program Manager, Robotics Data — Scale AI

Scale’s Robotics business unit is currently seeing historic levels of growth. As a Technical Program Manager (TPM) for Robotics Data, you will be the connective tissue across our most complex technical operations, driving initiatives that secure $XXM+ in new revenue.

This isn't a "sit back and watch" project management role. You will work with multiple project leads across a vast portfolio of robotics data projects, ensuring technical milestones are met, dependencies are de-risked, and stakeholders are aligned. You should be prepared to wear many hats and be excited about intense, impactful work that leads to accelerated career progression.

## You will:

- Drive Multi-Project Outcomes: Serve as the primary driver for a portfolio of robotics projects, tracking milestones, deliverables, and dependencies to ensure on-time delivery for the world’s leading AI researchers.

- Bridge the Technical-Operational Gap: Act as the central liaison between AI clients, hardware engineers, and global operational teams to translate high-level goals into executable data collection workflows.

- Architect Scalable Pipelines: Partner with engineering to design and optimize end-to-end data ingestion pipelines, ensuring they can handle the high-fidelity sensor data (video, logs, teleop) required for Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models.

- Enforce Data Quality & Governance: Define KPIs and Quality Assurance (QA) metrics to ensure datasets are high-fidelity, labeled correctly, and compliant with rigorous privacy and safety standards.

- Standardize for Scale: Develop and refine Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) that allow us to scale collection efforts across global, distributed teams.

- Communicate with Precision: Provide regular, data-driven progress updates to Scale’s executive team, utilizing SQL and dashboards to monitor performance and guide strategic pivots.

## Ideally, you’d have:

- Strong Technical Foundation: A degree in Engineering, Computer Science, or a related field, combined with a deep understanding of AI/ML.

- 2-3+ Years of TPM Experience: Proven track record of managing complex, cross-functional technical programs, ideally in robotics, hardware, or large-scale data operations.

- Hands-on Problem Solving Skills: A proactive approach to overcoming technical and logistical roadblocks in a fast-paced environment.

- Operational Grit: Experience "in the weeds" of operational challenges - you don’t just identify bottlenecks; you build the solution to clear them.

- Entrepreneurial Mindset: You are comfortable with ambiguity and excited about building infrastructure from scratch in a fast-paced environment.

## Nice to haves:

- SQL Proficiency: Ability to pull your own data and build your own tracking dashboards.

- Global Experience: Experience interacting with distributed teams or collection sites across different time zones.

- Robotics Domain Expertise: Familiarity with robotic hardware, sensor calibration, or the nuances of collecting data for Physical AI.

- AI/CV Knowledge: Familiarity with Computer Vision or Large Language Model (LLM) training requirements.
