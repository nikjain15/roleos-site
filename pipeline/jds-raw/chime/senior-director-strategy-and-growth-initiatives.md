# Senior Director, Strategy and Growth Initiatives — Chime

## About the role

We are hiring for a Sr. Director, Strategy and Growth Initiatives to help define and drive the long-term strategy and growth of the company. As our Sr. Director, you’ll identify where the business should go next, shape the long-term vision with senior leadership, and turn that into actionable initiatives that drive durable growth. You’ll work across Product, Marketing, Analytics, and other functions to solve the company’s most important and ambiguous problems, and ensure those strategies translate into real outcomes.

The base salary offered for this role and level of experience will begin at $253,000.00 and up to $350,000.00. Full-time employees are also eligible for a bonus, competitive equity package, and benefits. The actual base salary offered may be higher, depending on your location, skills, qualifications, and experience.

## In this role, you can expect to

- Identify and lead high-impact strategic initiatives that drive long-term growth and business durability

- Analyze market, competitor, and consumer trends to inform company strategy and priorities

- Partner closely with senior leaders to shape long-term vision and drive alignment across the organization

- Translate strategy into actionable plans and ensure initiatives are executed across cross-functional teams

- Develop deep analytical insights and research to support decision-making and prioritization

- Influence decisions and drive accountability across Product, Marketing, Analytics, and other functions

- Lead complex problem-solving efforts and guide project teams to deliver measurable business outcomes

## To thrive in this role, you have

- 10+ years of experience in strategy/management consulting, corporate strategy, or operating roles within financial services or fintech

- An MBA or equivalent experience

- Proven ability to influence senior stakeholders and drive alignment across complex organizationsx

- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills, including financial modeling and data-driven decision making

- Experience leading and managing project teams and driving initiatives end-to-end

- Excellent communication skills with the ability to translate insights into clear recommendations

- Deep experience in fintech/financial services and/or hands-on experience with data analysis, market research, or product analytics (preferred)

## A little about us

At Chime, we believe that everyone can achieve financial progress. We created Chime—a financial technology company, not a bank*—on the premise that core banking services should be helpful, easy, and free. Through our user-friendly tools and intuitive platforms, we empower our members to take control of their finances and work towards their goals. Whether it's starting a savings account, purchasing a first car or home, launching a business, or pursuing higher education, we're proud to have helped millions unlock their financial potential.

We're a team of problem solvers, dreamers, and builders with one shared obsession: our members. From day one, Chimers have worked tirelessly to out-hustle and out-execute competitors to bring our mission to life. Their grit and determination inspire us to work harder every day to deliver the very best experience possible. We each bring an owner's mindset to our work, refusing to be outdone and holding ourselves accountable to meet and exceed the highest bars for our teams, our company, and our members.

We believe in being bold, dreaming big, and taking risks, while also working together, embracing our diverse perspectives, and giving each other honest feedback. Our culture remains deeply entrepreneurial, encouraging every Chimer to see themselves as stewards of our mission to help everyday Americans unlock their financial progress. 

We know that to achieve our mission, we must earn and keep people's trust—so we hold ourselves to the highest standards of integrity in everything we do. These aren't just words on a wall—our values are embedded in every aspect of our business, serving as a north star that guides us as we work to help millions achieve their financial potential.

Because if we don't—who will?
