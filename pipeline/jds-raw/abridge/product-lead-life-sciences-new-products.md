# Product Lead, Life Sciences (New Products) — Abridge

## About Abridge
Abridge was founded in 2018 with the mission of powering deeper understanding in healthcare. Our AI-powered platform was purpose-built for medical conversations, improving clinical documentation efficiencies while enabling clinicians to focus on what matters most—their patients.

Our enterprise-grade technology transforms patient-clinician conversations into structured clinical notes in real-time, with deep EMR integrations. Powered by Linked Evidence and our purpose-built, auditable AI, we are the only company that maps AI-generated summaries to ground truth, helping providers quickly trust and verify the output. As pioneers in generative AI for healthcare, we are setting the industry standards for the responsible deployment of AI across health systems.

We are a growing team of practicing MDs, AI scientists, PhDs, creatives, technologists, and engineers working together to empower people and make care make more sense. We have offices located in the Mission District in San Francisco, the SoHo neighborhood of New York, and East Liberty in Pittsburgh. 

## About the Role
This is a zero-to-one role. You'll be the first PM for our life sciences product line, responsible for defining what we build, who we sell to, and how we commercialize. You'll work directly with Abridge's clinical, ML, and commercial teams. The right person has built in life sciences before.

This role will be a hybrid position. Candidates must be willing to work from our New York City Office or San Francisco Office 3 times a week or open to relocation. 

## What You'll Do
- Define the life sciences product strategy. Identify the highest-value use cases.

- Lead discovery with life sciences partners. Understand their workflows, data needs, buying processes, and willingness to pay

- Drive 0-to-1 product development. Define requirements, partner with ML and engineering to build the product.

- Navigate data strategy and compliance. Work with legal, clinical, and engineering to understand what data Abridge can use, under what conditions, and how to structure agreements that protect customer trust

- Be the internal expert on life sciences.

## What You Bring
- 5-10+ years of product management experience, with meaningful time spent building products for or within life sciences. Pharma, biotech, medical device, or health data and analytics companies.

- 0-to-1 experience. You’ve shipped new products.

- Commercial fluency. You've worked closely with sales, partnerships, or BD on early-stage deals

- Comfort with ambiguity. You're good at running discovery, forming hypotheses, and updating quickly when you're wrong.

- Strong cross-functional credibility. You can talk to ML scientists about model outputs, to lawyers about data use agreements, and to a pharma VP about their clinical development pipeline.

- Clear, direct communication. You write and speak well. You can take something complicated and make it simple without losing precision.

## Bonus Points
- Experience with real-world evidence, observational data, or clinical data platforms (e.g. Flatiron, Symphony Health, IQVIA).

- Familiarity with how pharma companies structure and fund digital health partnerships or data licensing deals.

- Prior work at the intersection of AI/ML and clinical data.

- Experience with IRB considerations, HIPAA, or de-identification frameworks.
