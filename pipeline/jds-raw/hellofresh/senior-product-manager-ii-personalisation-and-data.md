# Senior Product Manager II, Personalisation & Data — HelloFresh

Work with HelloFresh in Warsaw and its HelloTech organisation, HelloFresh’s global technology backbone with more than 1000 people, building the digital products that power our end-to-end food experience. From meal kits and ready-to-eat meals to specialty offerings like pet food and premium meat & seafood, HelloTech creates the platforms that bring tailored food solutions to millions of customers every month.

Our subscription-based, direct-to-consumer model relies on technology at every step, from customer-facing apps and personalization logic to pricing, forecasting, supply chain optimization, and initiatives that help reduce food waste. While our brands operate independently to serve distinct customer needs, they are united by shared platforms, data, and operational excellence built by HelloTech.

HelloTech works in autonomous, cross-functional alliances, each owning a specific product or domain end to end. By working with our Warsaw office, you will help shape scalable, data-driven products used across across our markets, working with a modern tech stack and international teams to continuously improve how people discover, order, and enjoy HelloFresh’s products, today and in the future.

## About the role: What's in the Box

The service provider will contribute to the "Personalized Chef" initiative, a mission-critical domain focused on matching global customers with tailored culinary experiences. This project involves providing expert product management services to bridge the gap between complex machine learning models and actionable data assets. The Senior Product Manager II will serve as a key consultant within a cross-functional setup, accelerating the validation of intelligent models and ensuring the delivery of a flawless, productized data foundation that powers personalization, marketing, and operational excellence across 18+ business units.

## What you’ll do: The Recipe

- Service Delivery for Data Foundations: Architect and deliver a strategy for Core Data Products, ensuring high-quality, connected data assets (customer profiles, subscription plans, order history) are ready for consumption by global stakeholders.

- Model Experimentation Management: Lead the iteration cycle for model testing, designing and executing complex A/B testing roadmaps to improve core metrics and reduce lead time to insights.

- AI-Enhanced Discovery: Integrate GenAI tools into the product discovery lifecycle to accelerate the synthesis of customer signals and the delivery of decision-ready technical specifications.

- Strategic Roadmap Execution: Translate high-ambiguity business requirements into long-term (15-month) technical roadmaps, operating with high autonomy to align data infrastructure with global commercial objectives.

- Cross-Functional Technical Coordination: Act as the primary point of contact between technical engineering squads and commercial business units to ensure data ecosystem connectivity.

## What you’ll bring: The Ingredients

- Data & Execution Expertise: 5+ years of experience in product management with a specific focus on data products, platforms, or ML-driven environments.

- Analytical Proficiency: Demonstrated ability to perform independent data analysis using SQL/Python and define robust input/output KPIs.

- Strategic Prioritization: Strong critical thinking skills used to identify high-impact model or data adjustments and manage stakeholder expectations.

- Technical Communication: Proficiency in translating complex concepts (e.g., data lineage, model cold-starts) into clear narratives for non-technical stakeholders.

- AI Integration: Proven experience incorporating AI-assisted workflows into daily professional practice to enhance output quality and discovery speed.

Above all, we are looking for individuals who will make HelloFresh better. We believe there are many different ways of developing skills and we love diverse experiences! So even if you don’t “tick all the boxes” but think you’d thrive in this role, we would really like to learn more about you.
