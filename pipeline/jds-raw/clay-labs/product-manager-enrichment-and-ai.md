## Product Manager, Enrichment & AI
We're looking for a Product Manager to own Enrichment—one of Clay's most critical and technically complex product areas. You'll lead strategy across tables, integrations, Claygent (our AI research agent), and our 3rd party data marketplace, partnering closely with engineering to ship fast and drive impact at scale.

As the PM for Enrichment, you'll shape the core workflows that power thousands of GTM teams daily. This role offers significant scope and a major AI component as we push the boundaries of AI-powered data enrichment and automated workflow building.

We strongly prefer candidates based in New York, as our product, engineering, and design teams collaborate in person.

## What You'll Do
- Own the vision, strategy, and roadmap for Enrichment—including tables, integrations, Claygent, and our data marketplace

- Lead core AI product initiatives, including using AI to automatically build tables and research workflows

- Partner with engineering to ship high-quality features across 5+ engineering teams

- Work cross-functionally with GTM and Solutions teams to ensure product-market fit at scale

- Balance performance, complexity, and user experience as we scale enrichment infrastructure

## What You'll Bring
- 7+ years of product management experience at high-growth B2B SaaS companies (1-100 or 100-1000 stage preferred)

- Experience owning complex, technical products—ideally data platforms, workflow automation, or developer tools

- Strong understanding of integrations, APIs, and how technical systems connect (CS degree not required, but you're a fast technical learner)

- Track record of driving cross-functional alignment across product, engineering, and go-to-market teams

- Excitement about AI's potential to transform how users interact with data
