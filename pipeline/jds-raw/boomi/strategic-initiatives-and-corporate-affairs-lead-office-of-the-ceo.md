How You’ll Make An Impact

Boomi is looking for a Strategic Initiatives & Corporate Affairs Lead to join the Office of the CEO. This role sits at the center of how Boomi operates and shows up to the market, supporting company narrative, advancing key initiatives, and strengthening how we engage with customers, partners, and key external audiences. 

Reporting to the VP, Office of the CEO & Corporate Affairs, this individual will partner closely with the Executive Leadership Team to help ensure Boomi’s strategy is clearly communicated, well-coordinated, and consistently reinforced across the business and in the market. 

This is a high-impact role for someone who thrives at the intersection of strategy, execution, and communication, and who is comfortable operating across a wide range of priorities in a fast-moving environment.

## What You Will Do

This individual will support the Office of the CEO across a range of evolving priorities. Responsibilities will flex based on business needs, but core areas include:

- Help run the Office of the CEO: Drive day-to-day coordination across priorities, support executive workflows, and ensure key initiatives, meetings, and follow-ups are well-structured and moving forward.

- Support Corporate Affairs Priorities: Help bring Boomi’s narrative and positioning to life across key audiences, ensuring clarity and consistency in how we communicate internally and externally.

- Drive Strategic Initiatives: Lead and support high-priority, cross-functional initiatives on behalf of the CEO and executive team, helping move work forward from concept through execution.

- Contribute to Market Engagement: Support how Boomi shows up with customers, partners, and the broader market through content, coordination, and preparation for key moments.

- Enable Executive Effectiveness: Assist in preparing for critical moments including executive communications, customer and partner engagements, and leadership forums.

- Support Operating Rhythm & Alignment: Help coordinate leadership cadences and forums to ensure alignment on priorities, decisions, and follow-through.

- Represent the Office of the CEO: Operate with a high level of discretion and judgment, effectively representing the Office of the CEO across internal and external interactions.

## The Experience You Bring

- 5–8 years of experience in strategy, operations, corporate affairs, or related roles within a high-growth SaaS or technology company.

- Experience working on cross-functional projects and supporting initiatives that span multiple teams.

- Strong communication skills, with experience developing content for senior leaders or executive audiences.

- Understanding of B2B technology businesses and how companies position and operate in competitive markets.

- Ability to work effectively with senior stakeholders and navigate ambiguity.

- Strong organizational skills and ability to manage multiple priorities at once.

- High level of discretion and professionalism in handling sensitive information.

- Willingness to learn, take ownership, and operate in a fast-paced environment.

- Willingness to travel up to 10% of the time.
