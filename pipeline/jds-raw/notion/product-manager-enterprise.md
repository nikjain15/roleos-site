# Product Manager, Enterprise — Notion

## About Us:
Notion helps you build beautiful tools for your life’s work. In today's world of endless apps and tabs, Notion provides one place for teams to get everything done, seamlessly connecting docs, notes, projects, calendar, and email—with AI built in to find answers and automate work. Millions of users, from individuals to large organizations like Toyota, Figma, and OpenAI, love Notion for its flexibility and choose it because it helps them save time and money.

In-person collaboration is essential to Notion's culture. We require all team members to work from our offices on Mondays, Tuesdays, and Thursdays, our designated Anchor Days. Certain teams or positions may require additional in-office workdays.

## About the Role:
As the Enterprise Product Manager at Notion, you will define how organizations adopt, scale, and succeed with Notion. You'll be responsible for making Notion a seamless, secure, and powerful tool for businesses—driving improvements in onboarding content, permissions, integrations, and overall ease of adoption. Your work will empower companies to confidently integrate Notion into their workflows and maximize its value across teams.

Notion is re-defining what Enterprise-grade means in an agentic world, this means instead of predictable release cycles, you’ll support rapid product development with controlled deployments. This means, instead of deterministic software, you’ll help Enterprises unleash the power from the latest models. This means instead of defining RBAC for people, you’ll be at the bleeding edge of defining how agent permissions scale alongside typical identity approaches.

## What You'll Achieve:
- Enterprise Strategy & Roadmap: Expand Notion’s integrations and extensibility features to enhance compatibility with enterprise ecosystems. Develop and execute a roadmap that empowers large organizations to onboard and scale with confidence.

- Enterprise Product Development: Work across multiple engineering teams to drive the scalable systems for user and agent management, team collaboration, and enterprise analytics to support successful adoption and ongoing management.

- Cross-functional Leadership: Be a champion for a seamless and delightful enterprise experiences across the company, ensuring the needs of security-minded organizations shape key product decisions. Partner closely with GTM teams to deeply understand business requirements, inform the product roadmap, and support strategic customer deals.

- Customer-Centric Development: Engage deeply with Notion’s admin and builder personas to understand their challenges, workflows, and aspirations. Use these insights to shape product priorities and drive solutions that make Notion an indispensable tool for enterprise teams.

## Skills You'll Need to Bring: 
- Track record of success: You have a track record of launching high quality B2B products that meaningfully impact company outcomes.

- Data Governance Background: You have fluency and point of view around access and governance for enterprise data.

- Collaborative mindset: You collaborate well cross-functionally—especially with GTM, engineering, design, and developer relations.

- Integration fluency: You have experience with with MCP servers, API development, and enterprise integrations.

- AI-pilled: You have a comfort and an ongoing curiosity with using AI tools for your product development process.

- Ambiguity and Pace Comfort: You have a proactive mindset even for ambiguous problems with a drive to deliver results with urgency throughout.

## Nice to Haves:

- You have a Computer Science background or equivalent technical experience.

- You care about having impact in a high-impact, high-agency, collaborative organization.
