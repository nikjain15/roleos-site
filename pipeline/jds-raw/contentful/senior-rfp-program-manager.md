# Senior RFP Program Manager — Contentful

## About the opportunity

The RFP Program Manager owns the end-to-end lifecycle of RFPs, RFIs, RFQs, and due diligence questionnaires. This role serves as the central coordinator for enterprise proposal development, ensuring high-quality, compliant, and timely responses aligned with company positioning and standards.

You will operate at the intersection of Sales, Deal Desk, Product, Legal, Security, and Finance, driving structured, scalable proposal execution.

## What to expect?

- Lead and project-manage the full RFP lifecycle from intake through submission

- Assess requirements and build structured response plans, timelines, and ownership

- Coordinate cross-functional inputs (Sales, Legal, Security, Product, Engineering, Finance)

- Draft, edit, and refine responses for clarity, accuracy, and consistency

- Ensure alignment with product positioning, commercial terms, and company standards

- Own, maintain and evolve the RFP knowledge base / content library

- Track deadlines, risks, approvals, and deliverables to ensure on-time submission

- Ensure compliance with technical, security, pricing, and contractual requirements

- Support post-submission activities (clarifications, best and final offers, follow-ups)

- Drive continuous improvement through metrics, reporting, and process enhancement

## What you need to be successful?

- 3–5+ years in RFP/proposal management, sales operations, or solution engineering

- Strong project management and organizational skills

- Excellent written communication skills and attention to detail

- Ability to operate cross-functionally in fast-paced, high-growth environments

- Familiarity with SaaS business models strongly preferred

- Experience with proposal tools (e.g., Loopio, RFPIO, Arphie) a plus

What success looks like

- High-quality, consistent, and on-time RFP submissions

- Scalable and well-maintained proposal content library

- Efficient cross-functional coordination with minimal friction

- Clear visibility into pipeline, timelines, and risks

- Continuous improvement in response quality and turnaround timing

Ideal profile

- Ownership mindset with strong attention to quality and deadlines

- Structured thinker who can manage complex, multi-stakeholder workflows

- Comfortable driving alignment across technical and business teams

- Balances speed with accuracy and compliance
