# Team Analyst – Global Focused Growth Strategy — T. Rowe Price

At T. Rowe Price, we identify and actively invest in opportunities to help people thrive in an evolving world. As a premier global asset management organization with more than 85 years of experience, we provide investment solutions and a broad range of equity, fixed income, and multi-asset capabilities to individuals, advisors, institutions, and retirement plan sponsors. We take an active, independent approach to investing, offering our dynamic perspective and meaningful partnership so our clients can feel more confident. 

We believe doing the right thing for our clients and our associates is good business. With a career at the firm, you can expect opportunities to create real impact at work and in your community. You’ll enjoy resources to support your career path, as well as compensation, benefits, and flexibility to enrich your life. Here, you’ll find a collaborative culture that respects and values differences and colleagues who share a spirit of generosity.  

Join us for the opportunity to grow and make a difference in ways that matter to you. 

Role Summary

- Supports Portfolio Manager Dave Eiswert in execution of investment process and management of a complex, diversified, global strategy.

- Investment research support for the Portfolio Manager Dave Eiswert within a diversified, global growth mandate.

- Analytical ownership of models, time-series analyses, and company/industry fundamentals.

- Generation of differentiated investment insights that influence positioning beyond pure “number crunching.”

- Direct engagement with public-company executives (in-office and in-field) to inform views.
Systematic use of AI tools and prompt engineering to accelerate and augment the research process.

- Apprenticeship with senior investors, building skills required for long-term advancement as a fundamental investor.

Responsibilities

- Adopt, execute, and continuously improve the Global Focused Growth investment framework (historical analysis, insight development, and forward modeling).

- Lead scoped company/industry research projects and thematic workstreams aligned to portfolio needs.

- Build and maintain three-statement and scenario models; synthesize market and alternative data into forecasts.

- Partner with the Portfolio Manager and senior analysts to prepare briefs, update views, and translate research into portfolio actions.

- Engage CEOs/CFOs and other external experts through meetings, calls, and road visits to test and refine theses.

- Extremely detail oriented documentation, compliance discipline, and data hygiene; contribute to reusable templates and best practices.

- Track ex-ante assumptions versus ex-post outcomes and participate in performance attribution reviews.

- Conduct primary diligence (filings, transcripts, channel checks, site visits) to deepen company and industry understanding.

- Develop investment recommendations over time and accept outcome accountability as responsibilities expand if appropriate.

- Design and apply AI-driven workflows (prompt libraries, retrieval, automation) to improve research speed and quality.

Qualifications

Required:

- Bachelor’s degree and 4+ years of relevant buy-side/sell-side investment experience.

- Advanced Excel and financial modeling capability, including three-statement builds and time-series analytics.

- Strong academic foundation combining liberal-arts reasoning with quantitative training (e.g., calculus, statistics, econometrics; CFA coursework).

- Excellent written and verbal communication.

- Working knowledge of equity markets and instruments.

- Proven facility with AI tools and prompt engineering for research use cases.

- Comfort operating in a high-intensity, performance-oriented team environment.

- Willingness to travel globally.

Preferred:

- CFA preferred

- Self-directed, confident contributor who works independently and collaborates effectively.

- Demonstrated passion for equity investing and market discovery.

- Track record of converting deep research into clear, actionable insights.
