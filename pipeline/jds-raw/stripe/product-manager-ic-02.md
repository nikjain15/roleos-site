# Product Manager IC-02 — Stripe

## Who we are

## About Stripe

Stripe, LLC. is a financial infrastructure platform for businesses. Millions of companies - from the world’s largest enterprises to the most ambitious startups - use Stripe to accept payments, grow their revenue, and accelerate new business opportunities. Our mission is to increase the GDP of the internet, and we have a staggering amount of work ahead. That means you have an unprecedented opportunity to put the global economy within everyone's reach while doing the most important work of your career.

## What you’ll do

## Responsibilities

- Define product strategy and lead cross-functional initiatives across reporting, data infrastructure and artificial intelligence to support internal teams at Stripe;

- Drive the development of internal GenAI applications, including use case scoping, vendor evaluation, data readiness, and change management for operational adoption;

- Conduct technical and business feasibility analysis for data center localization to support Stripe’s global expansion, addressing compliance, latency, and operational readiness; Lead efforts to platformize reporting by building reusable data models and internal tools that scale across multiple product and finance use cases;

- Oversee the development of a semantic layer to standardize key metrics and accelerate self-service analytics for data consumers;

- Collaborate closely with engineering, data science, legal, and compliance teams to ensure scalable, secure, and efficient product delivery.

## Who you are

## Minimum requirements

Bachelor’s degree or foreign equivalent in engineering or a related field, followed by 3 years of experience in product design or engineering.

Position also requires the following: 

- 
3 years of experience driving end-to-end product requirements across multiple teams;

- 
3 years of experience in gathering and prioritizing complex deliverables while engaging with customers, managing technical and business trade-offs, and collaborating in a cross-functional environment with customers, business leaders, and technical teams to deliver complex data and platform products in the fintech industry;

- 
3 years of experience working on user interface (UI) and user experience (UX) principles and best practices;

- 
3 years of experience with software product management;

- 
3 years of experience Utilizing data to manage metric, drive process improvement, and implement business cases;

- 
3 years of experience in developing company-wide product roadmaps, generating feature requirements, and executing go-to-market strategies; and

- 
2 years of experience building Gen AI applications for fintech industry.
