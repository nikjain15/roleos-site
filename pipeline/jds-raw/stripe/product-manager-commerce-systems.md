# Product Manager, Commerce Systems — Stripe

## Who we are

## About Stripe

Stripe is a financial infrastructure platform for businesses. Millions of companies—from the world’s largest enterprises to the most ambitious startups—use Stripe to accept payments, grow their revenue, and accelerate new business opportunities. Our mission is to increase the GDP of the internet, and we have a staggering amount of work ahead. That means you have an unprecedented opportunity to put the global economy within everyone’s reach while doing the most important work of your career. 

## About the team

The Commerce Systems team is at the literal nexus of Stripe’s expansion to a full-fledged Financial Services infrastructure platform. We own the internal revenue engine that enables every product team at Stripe to monetize their offerings, manage fees, and create complex commercial constructs. You will join a high-leverage 110-person organization, partnering with 10–20 engineers to harmonize disparate systems—from billing to financial data warehouses—into a single, elegant interaction surface.

## What you’ll do

As the PM for Commerce Systems, you will be the architect of the 'engine room' of Stripe’s revenue. You will be responsible for building the unified control planes that harmonize Stripe’s internal billing, seller systems, and financial data warehouses into a single, high-leverage interaction surface. In this role, your mission is to eliminate integration friction for internal product teams, allowing them to ship faster, while ensuring our merchants receive the world-class fee transparency and reconciliation tools they need to run their businesses.

## Responsibilities

- Harmonize Infrastructure: Build the "control planes" that unify seller systems, user billing, and financial data warehouses to reduce integration friction for product teams.

- Architect for Velocity: Drastically reduce the time-to-market for new Stripe products by streamlining monetization workflows and core underlying capabilities.

- Drive Strategy: Articulate the product vision for our revenue platform, developing annual and quarterly plans that align with Stripe’s rapid expansion into AI and new financial services.

- Navigate Complexity: Act as a leader and change agent, thriving in "chaos" to turn operational noise into a clear engineering roadmap.

- Cross-Functional Leadership: Partner with engineering leaders to design ingestion, enrichment, and serving interfaces while acting as a thought partner for GTM, Finance, and Legal.

## Who you are

We’re looking for someone who meets the minimum requirements to be considered for the role.

## Minimum requirements

- 7+ years of experience as a Product Manager in technology companies.

- Backend Foundation: Deep experience with large-scale backend systems and an API-first mindset.

- Platform Expertise: Proven track record building platform infrastructure that serves the needs of multiple disparate stakeholders.

- Communication: Ability to synthesize complex technical discussions and write succinctly for senior leadership. The ability to work cross functionally and speak multiple ‘languages’: you’re equally credible as a thought partner for Go-to-Market, Engineering, Finance or Legal leaders.

- Operational Resilience: Comfort dealing with many moving pieces and evolving legacy systems into their next generation.

- Most importantly, we’re looking for great product managers who deeply understand their users, turn that into great plans, and make those plans happen. Even if you don’t have all the experience above we’d encourage you to apply if this role excites you

Preferred Qualifications

- Experience in Fintech or at a Hyperscaler Cloud Infra provider (e.g., AWS, GCP, Azure).

- Experience managing technical software products from infancy to scale.

- A background in designing and building multi-product platforms or complex billing systems.
