# Program Manager, GTM Strategic Programs — Stripe

## Who we are 

## About Stripe

Stripe is a financial infrastructure platform for businesses. Millions of companies - from the world’s largest enterprises to the most ambitious startups - use Stripe to accept payments, grow their revenue, and accelerate new business opportunities. Our mission is to increase the GDP of the internet, and we have a staggering amount of work ahead. That means you have an unprecedented opportunity to put the global economy within everyone's reach while doing the most important work of your career.

## About the team

The Go-to-Market (GTM) Strategic Programs team supports Stripe’s mission (to ‘Increase the GDP of the Internet’) by building GTM strategy, bringing it to life through incubation programs and providing program management expertise across global, XFN programs. We bring a desire to chart the unknown, rigorous thinking, and a user-first approach to problem solving, while helping to build connections between Stripe go-to-market teams and the business overall.

As we rapidly grow our company and customer-base, we need ‘full stack’ program management experts with global program experience who easily flex between:

- PMO responsibilities coordinating across a number of cross-functional team members

- Running the full breadth of building and operationalizing incubation programs from diagnosis, design, implementation and change management 

- Driving a successful transition and handover to operational counterparts across GTM & Product Operations

## What you’ll do

Our Program Managers are focused on solving GTM’s most pressing challenges.  We are looking for a self-starter who has demonstrated success dealing with ambiguous global-scale programs, with multi-layered stakeholder management and significant cross-functional team engagement.  The ideal candidate will: (1) have worked in a hypergrowth technology/GTM org or comes from a strategic consulting background, (2) has superior communication skills to work across the most senior stakeholders across different functions, and (3) thrives when solving complex problems with limited oversight.

## Responsibilities

- Be accountable for building and driving large-scale programs end-to-end, and be directly responsible for marshaling the cross-functional support and resources needed to accomplish your goals

- Manage directly (or coordinate) the ‘full-stack’ of change management activities across stakeholder engagement and creating champions, messaging/communication planning, support and enablement

- Develop strong collaborative relationships with key stakeholders across Stripe across levels and functions

- Work cross-functionally to identify improvement opportunities for the Revenue teams spanning strategy, people, process, and technology

- Develop a Programs Center of Excellence, providing guidance, consulting, and coaching to peers and others on partner teams

- Seamlessly prioritize among various projects and synthesize data to guide/support strategy and decision

- Make C-suite level recommendations and drive decisions, where necessary

## Who you are

We're looking for someone who meets the minimum requirements to be considered for the role. If you meet these requirements, you are encouraged to apply. The preferred qualifications are a bonus, not a requirement.

## Minimum requirements

- Experience working in a sales and marketing organization 

- 7+ years of experience in program management, business operations, management consulting or a related discipline

- A strong track record of delivering global, complex programs with multiple constituents in a changing, ambiguous environment end-to-end

- Has an enthusiastic “roll up your sleeves” mentality

- Works well under pressure, with a high degree of adaptability and flexibility in a fast paced, rapidly changing environment.

- The ability to diagnose, simplify and structure problems and solutions while having command of “the details”

- Excellent analytical, presentation, and communication experience, influencing across all levels from front lines to C-Suite. You set a high-bar in terms of communication effectiveness

- Someone who demonstrates leadership qualities throughout their work – taking ownership of challenges, selflessly supporting their colleagues, and setting the standard for quality in their deliverables

## Preferred qualifications

- Comfort operating with incomplete data and adapting / re-prioritizing activities as business needs change

- Deep problem solving and analysis experience (solving business problems - commercial, operational, financial) in a Sales-oriented environment

- A growth mindset and innate curiosity where every interaction is an opportunity to learn and improve (yourself, and those around you)

- Prior experience with payments

- Comfort with SQL
