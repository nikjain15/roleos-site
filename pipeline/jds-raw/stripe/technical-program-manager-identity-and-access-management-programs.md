## What you’ll do

We are seeking an experienced Technical Program Manager to join our Organization Access Platform (OAP) team, focusing on security initiatives. In this role, you will drive critical security programs across multiple teams, manage dependencies, and ensure the successful delivery of security features that protect our platform and users. This is a highly technical role requiring strong program management skills and the ability to influence cross-functional stakeholders.

Your role involves spearheading large-scale technical programs, solving intricate challenges, and ensuring successful product launches.

## Responsibilities

- Lead end-to-end program management for critical security initiatives including API key security, Account Takeover (ATO) prevention, login access controls, and other risk/security/fraud prevention measures

- Drive cross-team dependencies and collaboration between security, fraud operations, and product engineering teams

- Track, communicate, and resolve program blockers across multiple workstreams

- Develop implementation roadmaps and execution strategies for complex security features

- Partner with engineering managers to define technical requirements and prioritize security enhancements

- Establish metrics and reporting processes to measure program effectiveness and security outcomes

- Facilitate decision-making processes when trade-offs are required between security, user experience, and development timelines

- Represent OAP security initiatives in cross-functional meetings and leadership reviews
