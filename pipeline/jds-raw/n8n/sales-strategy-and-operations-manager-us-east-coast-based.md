I This is an IC role I

🎯 Your main goal will be to enable scalable, data-driven GTM growth in AMER by driving strategic planning, sales segmentation, and continuous process improvements.

To do so, you’ll partner closely with AMER Sales leadership and the GTM Systems & Automation team to deliver data-driven insights, streamline operations, and execute initiatives that help the region hit ambitious growth targets.

## STRATEGIC PLANNING & ANALYSIS
- Partner with the VP of US Sales to develop regional sales strategies, quotas, and territory plans.

- Analyze sales data to generate actionable insights and drive performance improvement.

- Translate data into strategic recommendations that inform revenue targets and growth initiatives.

## FORECASTING & REGIONAL OPERATING CADENCE
- Support accurate forecasting, pipeline management, and performance reviews across the AMER region.

- Participate in the regional operating cadence and ensure alignment with global RevOps processes.

- Design and implement scalable reporting frameworks to track regional sales performance.

## PROCESS IMPROVEMENT & OPTIMIZATION
- Continuously improve sales and RevOps processes for efficiency, consistency, and scalability.

- Collaborate with the GTM Systems & Automation team to identify automation opportunities and implement best practices.

- Lead initiatives to optimize tools, workflows, and enablement processes for regional teams.

## PROGRAM MANAGEMENT & CROSS-FUNCTIONAL INITIATIVES
- Lead and coordinate cross-functional GTM initiatives that drive operational excellence.

- Ensure alignment across Sales, RevOps, Finance, and Systems teams on key projects.

- Manage programs from concept to execution, ensuring deliverables are on time and measurable.

## REQUIREMENTS

## Must-haves
- 📈 Revenue Operations experience: You have 5+ years in Revenue Operations, Sales Operations, or GTM operations roles, ideally in B2B SaaS.

- 🌍 US/AMER expertise: You’ve supported AMER sales teams or multinational GTM operations and understand the regional nuances.

- 🧠 Analytical mindset: You make data-driven decisions, leveraging analytics and forecasting to inform strategy.

- 🤝 Stakeholder collaboration: You build trusted partnerships with Sales, Finance, and cross-functional teams to deliver impactful outcomes.

- ⚙️ Systems proficiency: You’re comfortable with Salesforce (admin level), Google Sheets, and automation tools like n8n.

## Nice-to-haves
- 📊 Data visualization skills: You can build dashboards or reports in Tableau, Looker, or Power BI.

- 💡 Automation mindset: You’re passionate about using AI and automation to streamline RevOps workflows.

- 📚 Technical depth: You have working knowledge of SQL or similar analytical tools.

- 🚀 Scale-up experience: You’ve operated in high-growth, fast-scaling B2B SaaS environments.
