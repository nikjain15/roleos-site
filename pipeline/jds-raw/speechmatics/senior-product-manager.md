# Senior Product Manager — Speechmatics

The Role

As a Senior Product Manager, you will play a key role in driving the growth of Speechmatics’ Voice AI portfolio by setting product strategy and direction. You’ll work closely with customers, partners, and internal teams to deeply understand market needs, define clear requirements, and deliver solutions that create meaningful impact across industries.

In this role, you’ll also act as a product evangelist, promoting the value of Voice AI both internally and externally, while prioritizing initiatives that balance customer outcomes, technical feasibility, and business goals. By collaborating with engineering and go-to-market teams, you’ll shape features, guide development, launch new products, and iterate quickly to achieve measurable success.

This position is both strategic and collaborative, requiring vision as well as execution. You’ll transform customer insights into product roadmaps, align cross-functional stakeholders, and help accelerate the global adoption of Speechmatics’ Voice AI technology.

What you’ll be doing:

- Leading the strategy and execution of our world beating Voice AI products from development to GTM.

- Igniting enthusiasm internally and externally about what we are doing and why.

- Helping us scale at pace by accelerating our Product Led Growth with developers, implementing GTM engineering approaches and building momentum in our ICPs.

- Effectively gathering market, customer and technical understanding and driving the product roadmap based on this.

Who we are looking for:

- Full spectrum product veteran with multiple examples of leading cross functional teams to achieve material business success, ideally in tech scale-ups.

- Results driven and execution focused – enjoys forging a path through an ambiguous and rapidly evolving environments.

- Insatiably curiosity with a first principles approach to product growth.

- Bright but has humility – you’ll fit in well!

- Innovator, in technology and GTM, you love delivering ideas whose time has come.

- AI / ML literate - understands LLM limits, evals, performance/latency/cost trade-offs, data pipelines, guardrails, and can quickly gain respect of Eng/Research. Specific Voice AI experience certainly helps!

We encourage you to apply even if you do not feel you match all of the requirements exactly. The list of requirements is intended to show the kinds of experience and qualities we’re looking for, but it is not exhaustive. If you are interested in the role, the team, and our mission, we would love to consider your application. We are always open to conversations and look forward to hearing from you.

Who we are:

Speechmatics is the leading expert in Speech Intelligence, and uses AI and Machine Learning to unlock business value in human speech worldwide. We work with an amazing mix of global companies, and our technology can integrate into our customers stack irrespective of their industry or use case – making it the go-to solution to harness useful information from speech.

Joining us means working with some of the smartest minds around the world, focused on cutting-edge projects and deploying the latest techniques to disrupt the market. We believe in putting people first; we’ll do all we can to help you develop your skills and give you the tools you need to thrive. Our Focus Fridays give you an undisturbed day of focus, offset with Together Tuesdays when we have our team meetings, so you've always got the right balance.

We have structured a hybrid approach that includes 2-3 designated office days each week. This arrangement ensures that while we embrace the advantages of remote work, we also maintain the vital connection and synergy that only in-person interactions can foster.

This is only the beginning; we’re looking for amazing people like you to continue our journey…

What we can offer you:

No matter what stage of your career you’re at - from paid internships and first-job opportunities through to management and senior positions - we’ll support you with the training and development needed to reach your career aspirations with us. There really is no shortage of opportunities here for you to get involved and collaborate with those around you to deliver your best work.

We offer incredibly flexible working, regular company lunches, and birthday celebrations. But that’s not all. We’ve spoken to our teams to find out what they want.
