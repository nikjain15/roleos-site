# Senior Technical Program Manager, Privacy — Robinhood

## Join us in building the future of finance.

Our mission is to democratize finance for all. An estimated $124 trillion of assets will be inherited by younger generations in the next two decades. The largest transfer of wealth in human history. If you’re ready to be at the epicenter of this historic cultural and financial shift, keep reading.

## About the team + role

We are building an elite team, applying frontier technologies to the world’s biggest financial problems. We’re looking for bold thinkers. Sharp problem-solvers. Builders who are wired to make an impact. Robinhood isn’t a place for complacency, it’s where ambitious people do the best work of their careers. We’re a high-performing, fast-moving team with ethics at the center of everything we do. Expectations are high, and so are the rewards.

The Privacy team builds a scalable, risk-aware privacy program embedded across our technology, operations, and company practices. The team focuses on integrating privacy into product design, strengthening data governance, and ensuring global compliance while maintaining customer trust. You’ll work closely with engineering, legal, compliance, and data teams to ensure privacy is consistently applied across systems and processes. The team values clear communication, thoughtful problem solving, and measurable program execution.

As a Senior Technical Program Manager, Privacy, you will define and drive large-scale privacy initiatives across engineering teams. You will build program structure, establish milestones, and track measurable outcomes such as KPIs and delivery timelines. This role requires strong technical understanding to translate complex systems into actionable plans, along with the ability to communicate clearly with senior leaders. You will help shape how privacy programs are designed and executed across the company while ensuring alignment with regulatory and business needs!

This role is based in our New York, NY office, with in-person attendance expected at least 3 days per week.

At Robinhood, we believe in the power of in-person work to accelerate progress, spark innovation, and strengthen community. Our office experience is intentional, energizing, and designed to fully support high-performing teams.

## What you’ll do

- Define and manage privacy program roadmaps, including milestones, timelines, and measurable outcomes such as KPIs and reporting metrics

- Partner with engineering, legal, compliance, and data teams to ensure privacy requirements are clearly defined and implemented in systems and processes

- Build and scale programs that support data governance, including data lifecycle management, retention, and access controls

- Lead planning and reporting processes, including executive updates and progress tracking across multiple initiatives

- Translate complex technical systems into clear plans and documentation to support consistent execution across teams

## What you bring

- 5+ years of experience in technical program management or a related role within a technology-focused organization

- Ability to understand system design concepts and break down complex technical problems into structured plans

- Strong written and verbal communication skills, with the ability to present clearly to senior stakeholders

- Experience building and managing privacy programs with defined milestones, metrics, and reporting processes

- Ability to work through ambiguity, prioritize effectively, and deliver results in a high-growth environment
