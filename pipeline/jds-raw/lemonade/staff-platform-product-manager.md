# Staff Platform Product Manager — Lemonade

We're looking for a Staff Product Manager to define the long-term vision and strategy for LoCo - Lemonade's next-generation, AI-powered platform that's rebuilding how insurance products are built and launched. Sitting within our core platform team, this role is central to how Lemonade builds, ships, and scales insurance products across the org - and how we continue doing it faster with AI at the center.

We believe three things matter for every role at Lemonade: drive to push through challenges, efficiency that keeps standards high while moving fast, and adaptability that lets you pivot with data and AI insights. These aren't buzzwords, they're how we actually work.

Our AI-first approach isn't just a tagline either. We're building the future of insurance with AI at the center, and we need people who are genuinely excited to learn and grow alongside these tools.

## In this role you'll
- Define the architecture that determines how Lemonade builds, ships, and scales - for years to come

- Drive cross-team alignment on complex technical decisions, turning competing priorities into a clear, executable roadmap

- Own the long-term API strategy across multiple domains, creating a unified layer that simplifies onboarding for channel teams and scales with the business

- Act as the translation layer between platform engineers and leadership, communicating technical complexity in business terms

- Leverage AI to identify patterns, pressure-test architectural decisions, and unlock faster paths to solving platform-scale problems

- Own the adoption strategy across product lines, delivering a developer experience that makes our platform the obvious choice

## What you'll need
- 8+ years of product management experience building platform products at scale, with a track record of thinking architecturally and owning products from 0 to 1

- Proven ability to define product vision, build roadmaps from scratch, and make the hard calls on what to build and why

- Hands-on with AI tools - you don't just follow trends, you actively use AI to move faster and make better product decisions

- Experience taking products from 1 to a billion, with a track record of overseeing complex adoption and integration efforts

- Cross-team leadership experience with a history of aligning stakeholders and driving complex, multi-team initiatives forward

- A computer science degree or substantial hands-on development experience

- Ready to work in an office environment most days of the week
