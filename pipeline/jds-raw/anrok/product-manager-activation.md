# Product Manager, Activation — Anrok

Anrok is the leading tax automation platform enabling businesses to expand globally without compliance complexity.

As the digital economy has grown 6x over the last decade, software businesses have gone from not worrying about sales tax to needing to monitor exposure, calculate rates, and file returns across 50 US jurisdictions and 100+ countries. This creates a critical bottleneck for companies that should be able to transact with customers everywhere.

Anrok eliminates this complexity by connecting with billing and payment systems to automate tax monitoring, calculations, and filing end-to-end. Our unified platform handles the ever-changing maze of tax laws at municipal, state, and federal levels—so companies can focus on growth, not compliance.

Our customers include:

- 40% of Forbes Top 50 AI companies

- 20% of Forbes Top 100 Cloud companies

- Top companies like Notion, Anthropic, and Cursor

We're making compliant digital commerce a reality for companies big and small, backed by over $100M from leading investors including Sequoia, Spark, Index, and Khosla Ventures.

As the Activation Product Manager, you’ll own a critical piece of the customer journey: built and lead our product-led growth motion. Today, thousands of early-stage companies discover Anrok, but our sales-assisted onboarding can’t scale with growing volume. We're leaving growth on the table. Our vision is for Anrok to be in every company's founding stack: you get incorporated in Delaware, buy Rippling, buy Anrok.

We need someone to transform our onboarding experience from sales-assisted to self-serve so companies can get compliant in minutes, not days — and then grow with us into becoming the next generation of enterprise customers.

## In this role you will
- Make our onboarding process self-serve and the fastest on the market

- Automate our tax registrations process so customers get compliant quickly

- Define and scale product-led acquisition channels

- Identify the next frontier of product and business opportunities for Anrok

## What excites us
- Have 6+ years product management experience, 3+ years building B2B products

- Are excited by bringing clarity to ambiguous problems

- Enjoy wearing different hats in a lean environment

- Think like a GM with strong product and business instincts

- Communicate clearly and concisely, both verbally and in writing

- Will do what it takes to make your product and team successful
