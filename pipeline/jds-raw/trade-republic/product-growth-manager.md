# Product Growth Manager — Trade Republic

Please note that this position is based in Berlin, Germany - relocation support is provided if required.

 

 

## THE BEST WORK OF YOUR CAREER

Trade Republic is the largest savings platform in Europe - we operate in 18 countries, serving +10 million customers who trusted us with over 150B in assets. But we’re striving for more.

 

We have a bold mission to empower everyone to build wealth with easy, safe, and free access to financial systems. You will have the opportunity to grow your career by collaborating with a team of outstanding talents and state of the art technology to build a lasting, positive future for millions.

 

 

## WHAT YOU’LL BE DOING

As a Product Growth Manager, you will be owning the intersection between our Product and Growth (Internationalization & Marketing) teams. Your responsibilities will mostly be to drive adoption of existing and new products by:

- Developing the customer facing communication and launch strategy for new and existing products

- Coordinating execution of launches across our global growth channels (paid marketing, press, engagement and own social media) across our 17 markets

- Collaborating closely with the product management and operations team to funnel back customer insights

- Driving data analysis to inform USP, communication, creative and budget decisions within the growth organization as well as tracking performance of campaigns

##  

## 
WHAT WE'RE LOOKING FOR

- Minimum 2 years of experience in a high pace, analytical environment such as tier 1 consulting, investment banking or growth stage startups

- Excellent project management skills with the ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously and work effectively with cross-functional teams at all levels of the organization

- Strong communication and analytical skills with the ability to translate data into actionable insights and recommendations

- Being a power user of our App and excited about financial markets, banking and financial education
