# Senior Manager, Consumer Discovery - Strategy & Operations — DoorDash

## About the Team

The Consumer Discovery Team is the heart of how our customers engage with DoorDash in our app. We’re focused on building and scaling new features, bringing products to market and truly turning DoorDash into the everyday app for customers. The Consumer Discovery Team supports the entire marketplace - across Restaurants, New Verticals and Emerging Bets.

## About the Role

As a Senior Manager of the Consumer Discovery Strategy & Operations team, you will be responsible for defining, executing and building in-app experiences for our entire marketplace. From bringing AI-forward solutions into our app to refining our go-to-market playbook, you’ll work cross-functionally across other teams within strategy & operations, product, engineering, analytics, marketing and so many others! You will manage a team of direct and indirect reports and report into a Director on our Marketplace Strategy & Operations team.

## You’re excited about this opportunity because you will…

- Build. You’ll have your fingerprints all over our consumer app - you’ll do whatever it takes to launch, get 1% better every day, and scale something completely new. This means everything from high-level strategy to nitty-gritty operational details. 

- Strategize. From first principles, you’ll develop the optimal strategy for best serving our customers. 

- Experiment. You’ll transform strategies into operational experiments that create measurable and actionable outcomes, informing your larger initiative implementation plans.

- Analyze. You’ll assess the operational and financial impacts of your decisions, and iterate based on data.

- Lead and collaborate. You’ll work with a cross-functional team, including marketing, design, product, and operations.

## We’re excited about you because…

- You solve problems from first principles. You’re excited to solve problems in innovative ways, and you resist “this is how it’s always been done” thinking.

- You’re customer focused. You are able to identify consumer pain points, lead with the consumer front of mind and build go to market strategy to enhance our consumer experience. 

- You’re an AI leader. You’ve built, launched, or scaled AI-powered consumer products, and you can help raise the bar for how our team builds, makes decisions and creates more magical customer experiences.

- You’re equally comfortable operating at 10,000 feet and 1 foot. You don’t hesitate to get in the weeds and operate at the lowest level of detail, but you’re just as comfortable thinking long-term and inspiring a team. No job is beneath you.

- You’re analytical. You let data win arguments, and you’re comfortable pulling your own data in SQL or modeling in Excel.

- You’re unafraid of ambiguity. Rapid change and learning is exciting to you, and you thrive in ambiguous, startup environments

- You have 8+ years of experience. You’ve succeeded in high-performance cultures (whether in tech, operations, strategy, consulting, banking, startups or a related experience).

- You are a leader. You have 2+ years of experience managing teams.

## About DoorDash

At DoorDash, our mission to empower local economies shapes how our team members move quickly, learn, and reiterate in order to make impactful decisions that display empathy for our range of users—from Dashers to merchant partners to consumers. We are a technology and logistics company that started by enabling door-to-door delivery, and we are looking for team members who can help us go from a company that is known as the place you order food to a company that people turn to for any and all goods.

DoorDash is growing rapidly and changing constantly, which gives our team members the opportunity to share their unique perspectives, solve new challenges, and own their careers. We're committed to supporting employees’ happiness, healthiness, and overall well-being by providing comprehensive benefits and perks including premium healthcare, wellness expense reimbursement, paid parental leave and more.
