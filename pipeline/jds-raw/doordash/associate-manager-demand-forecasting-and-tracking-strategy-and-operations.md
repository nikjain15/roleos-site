# Associate Manager, Demand Forecasting and Tracking - Strategy & Operations — DoorDash

## About the Team

Our Advertising & Promotion team operates at the intersection of e-commerce and advertising, surfacing and monetizing relevant content that benefits our core audiences. We’ve built a retail media network that enables CPG advertisers to reach DoorDash audiences and drive sales and category growth. This role will help accelerate CPG Ads growth by building and scaling net-new demand channels through strategic partnerships.

## About the Role

You will report into the Director of Go-To-Market and Demand on our CPG Ads team within Ads Strategy & Operations. In this role, you will own the strategy and creation of our partner demand and forecasting capabilities. You will:

- Own Partner GMV Forecasting: Leading the development of our capabilities to accurately project CPG partner growth that will occur on the DoorDash platform. 

- Own JBP Scenario Planning: Empowering our sales and partnerships team to effectively model different outcome scenarios based on different investment decisions based on CPG partnership goals and objectives 

- Establish Demand Tracking Capabilities: Help the team to diagnose business performance and what is driving weekly, monthly, quarterly and annual performance vs plan. 

- Support our Internal Planning Process: Work cross functionally with finance and sales operations to help ensure that we are building the most accurate and effective internal revenue goals.

## You’re excited about this opportunity because you can…

- Own: You are energized by building new tools and processes to help internal teams be more effective and efficient. 

- Data Mastery: You enjoy working across multiple data sources, synthesizing various data sources and leveraging them to build accurate revenue forecasts. 

- Problem-solve: You thrive in ambiguity and turn constraints into solutions.

- Collaborate: You excel at influencing cross-functionally and aligning stakeholders on projects that require input from multiple teams. 

- Enable: You enjoy translating sales team needs and pain points into solutions that can be built to enable them to be successful. 

## We’re excited about you because…

- You have 4+ years of experience in retail, CPG or media forecasting and analytics. 

- You have a track record of building scalable forecasting tools that empower internal teams to be successful. 

- You bring strong business and analytical rigor, with advanced SQL and analytics experience. 

- You are comfortable using AI tools to accelerate your output (i.e. Cursor, Claude Code) 

- Bachelor’s degree required.
