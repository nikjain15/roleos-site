# Associate Manager, New Verticals - Selection Strategy & Operations — DoorDash

## About the Team

New Verticals is an exciting team at DoorDash that is building and scaling new ways to delight our customers beyond restaurant delivery.

Our Catalog, Fulfillment and Logistics team has three major mandates to effectively onboard merchants, fulfill every order with speed & accuracy and improve our logistics network. We are able to do this by identifying efficiencies, building product toolings and launching operational processes at scale. You will own metrics, pull operational & product levers and drive impactful results across our Grocery, Convenience, Alcohol and Retail businesses.

## About the Role

We are looking to hire an Associate Manager on the New Verticals Fulfillment pod. You will lead the scoping, go-to-market, and iteration of cutting-edge tech solutions to help DoorDash improve selection coverage across our Convenience, Grocery, Alcohol, and Retail businesses. You will work on initiatives that address selection gaps to improve the way we manage and predict inventory through machine learning. You will partner with the Machine Learning, Engineering, Product, and Data Science teams. You will sit in Catalog, Fulfillment, and Logistics group within our New Verticals Strategy & Operations organization and report into a Manager. This is an individual contributor position.  

## You’re excited about this opportunity because you will…

- Analyze. You love working with data to drive business decision-making and are familiar with SQL.

- Lead: You'll spearhead cross-functional projects, collaborating with Engineers, Data Scientists, and Product Managers to develop and implement tech solutions that increase selection across our New Vertical offerings.

- Strategize: You will own and deliver on improving key selection metrics. You’ll create and execute quarterly and annual strategic plans aligned with product capabilities to elevate order quality. 

- Build: You will build strategic initiatives and drive innovation across our offerings; this can vary from AI-powered selection predictions to improving the Cx shopping experience. This means everything from high-level strategy to operational details.

## We’re excited about you because…

- You are an operator. You don’t hesitate to get in the weeds and operate at the lowest level of detail, but you’re also able to think long-term. You are excited to experience our operation firsthand to inform your strategy.

- You solve problems from first principles. You’re excited to think innovatively, and you resist “this is how it’s always been done” thinking.

- You’re analytical. You possess a sharp analytical mind, and can extract and communicate meaningful insights from data to drive strategic decisions. You are comfortable with SQL and data visualization tools.

- You have 4-6+ years of experience. You’ve succeeded in high-performing cultures (tech, operations, strategy, consulting, banking, startups or a related experience). 

- You have a completed Bachelor’s Degree.
