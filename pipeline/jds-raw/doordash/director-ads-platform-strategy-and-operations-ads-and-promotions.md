# Director, Ads Platform Strategy & Operations, Ads & Promotions — DoorDash

## About the Team

Our Advertising & Promotions team operates at the intersection of e-commerce and advertising, surfacing and monetizing relevant content that benefits our core audiences.  We provide digital solutions to help advertisers reach DoorDash audiences. 

The Ads Platform Strategy & Operations team is responsible for: 1) Driving sales and campaign lifecycle efficiency via automation and process optimization, 2) Building the platform foundation to help us scale, and 3) Creating simple self-serve tools for merchants to drive GMV growth.

## About the Role

We're looking for a senior builder and operator who is passionate about working across teams to develop systems, processes, and tools that will enable our advertising business to continue scaling through a period of hypergrowth. As the Director of Ads Platform Strategy & Operations, you will own the overall vision, strategy, and execution of all tools and systems that support our advertising business — with a particular emphasis on leveraging Gen AI to revolutionize how we sell, build, and optimize campaigns.

Specifically, you will: 

- Own the overall vision, strategy, and execution of the Ads Platform S&O roadmap, covering the full sales and campaign lifecycle — from proposal and contracting through execution, invoicing, and reporting.

- Champion and lead the integration of Gen AI across our ads workflows, identifying high-leverage opportunities to drive scalability across the org.

- Partner with Sales and merchant teams to identify gaps in existing workflows; and implement, manage, and scale sales and operations via offshore resources, automation tools, and streamlined end-to-end processes.

- Work closely with Product and Engineering to create and execute quarterly roadmaps focused on infrastructure, tooling, and automation.

- Oversee Ad Operations and manage team leads to scale operations, grow offshore resources, and improve team SLAs.

- Work with internal teams (Billing, Accounting, Finance, Compliance, Legal) to support scaled ways to propose, contract, execute, and invoice campaigns.

- Use data-driven decision-making to run and scale experiments — from new engagement models for Sales to new self-serve tools for Merchants — to drive Ads growth.

- Build systems to improve quality of campaign delivery and overall excellence of what we deliver for our advertisers.

## You’re excited about this opportunity because you will…

- Build: You are eager to implement efficiency, automation, and best practices — and excited to explore the best ways to leverage Gen AI that will unlock our ability to scale.

- Analyze: You use data-driven decision-making to design, run, and scale experiments and operational improvements that move the business forward.

- Operate: You want to roll up your sleeves, get to the lowest level of detail, and find the best solution in practice — not just in theory.

- Collaborate: You value cross-functional partnerships and will thrive working across Sales, Operations, Insights, Product, Engineering, Legal, Accounting, and more to deliver results.

- Strategize: You'll develop the strategy and roadmap to best serve our advertisers and internal partners through a period of rapid growth.

## We’re excited about you because…

- You have 10+ years of experience in strategy & operations, product operations, product management, business operations, management consulting, or ad operations — experience in advertising or ad tech is strongly preferred.

- You have a proven track record of leading highly complex, cross-functional projects and delivering results on time.

- You have deep expertise in process design and optimization, with a passion for operational efficiency and a willingness to get into the details.

- You have a strong interest in, or direct experience with, leveraging AI and other emerging technologies to solve real business problems.

- You don't need a playbook. You dive into difficult, ambiguous problems and develop a clear plan forward.

- You are a strong leader who has managed direct reports and teams, and who gets energized by building and developing talent.

- You thrive while wearing multiple hats and working across a variety of high-visibility projects and workstreams simultaneously.

- You have exceptional analytical skills (Excel / Google Sheets / SQL) and are proficient — and a fast learner — across a variety of tech platforms such as Salesforce, Snowflake, and internal tools.
