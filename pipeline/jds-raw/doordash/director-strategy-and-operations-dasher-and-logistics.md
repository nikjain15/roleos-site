# Director, Strategy & Operations - Dasher and Logistics — DoorDash

## About the Team

The Dasher & Logistics team ensures every delivery is completed with speed, accuracy, and a positive experience for consumers, merchants, and Dashers. They own the health of our delivery marketplace — including cost, quality, efficiency, fulfillment, and sustainable growth. The team partners closely with Product, Engineering, Finance, and Operations to improve how our logistics system works at scale while supporting long-term marketplace health.

## About the Role

We’re hiring a Director, Strategy & Operations to lead one of the most important business areas within Dasher & Logistics. In this role, they will set the strategy and own results across cost management, incremental revenue, delivery quality, efficiency, and/or fulfillment capacity. They will translate company goals into clear, measurable plans, lead a team of senior managers and operators, and serve as the decision-maker for their business area. Success in this role means delivering durable business impact while building a high-performing and inclusive organization.

You will report into a Senior Director on our Dasher & Logistics team in our Strategy & Operations organization. We expect this role to be hybrid with some time in-office and some time remote.

## You’re excited about this opportunity because you will…

- Own end-to-end business outcomes, including cost per order, delivery quality, efficiency, fulfillment rate, and/or incremental revenue generation

- Define the strategy for your organization and translate it into clear goals, measurable milestones, and accountable owners

- Partner cross-functionally with Product, Engineering, Finance, and Operations leaders to align on priorities and deliver shared outcomes

- Serve as the escalation point for complex issues, proactively identify risks, and adjust plans to keep goals on track

- Build and develop a team of leaders, creating clarity, accountability, and strong engagement across your organization

## We’re excited about you because…

- You have 10+ years of experience in strategy, operations, consulting, technology, finance, or a related field, and experience leading managers or senior leaders

- You have owned large, complex business areas with clear accountability for financial and operational results

- You have experience building data-driven strategies and translating insights into measurable execution plans

- You are comfortable influencing senior executives and aligning cross-functional partners around shared goals

- You have experience building and developing high-performing, inclusive teams
