# Associate, Merchant Strategy & Operations — DoorDash

## About the Team

The Merchant is at the core of DoorDash. The Merchant Team powers many facets of our business and is one of the primary drivers of growth and revenue. On the Merchant Experience Strategy & Ops team, our mission is to have best-in-class merchant experience and build cutting edge tools & workflows for our merchants to grow their business and operate seamlessly. 

## About the Role

We are looking for an operator with a strong bias to action who loves using data to solve problems. You’ll join the Live Order Apps & Tablet team, where we’re focused on improving the tools and technology merchants use to manage incoming DoorDash orders through hardware, software, and AI-powered operational workflows. In this role, you’ll develop go-to-market strategies, launch experiments and pilots, and support growth initiatives that expand merchant adoption of live order management tools and tablet hardware. You’ll work cross-functionally across Product, Engineering, Analytics, Operations, Procurement, Sales, and B2B Marketing teams to help build and scale foundational merchant technology experiences.

We’re looking for someone who is self-motivated, highly analytical, data-driven, and comfortable operating in ambiguity, with exceptional interpersonal, communication, and stakeholder management skills.

## You’re excited about this opportunity because you will…

- Operate & Build – Drive strategy and execution across high-impact merchant technology and operational initiatives, helping shape the future of DoorDash’s live order management and hardware ecosystem at scale

- Analyze – Build structured analyses and business cases to evaluate operational performance, merchant adoption, and the impact of strategic pilots and product launches

- Experiment – Use data-driven decision-making to scope, run, and scale pilots and experiments that improve merchant workflows and reduce operational friction

- Influence & Work Cross-Functionally – Partner closely with Product, Engineering, Analytics, Operations, Procurement, Sales, B2B Marketing, and international teams to drive alignment and execution on complex initiatives

- Make Stuff Happen – Thrive in ambiguity and fast-paced environments by bringing structure to complexity, maintaining exceptional attention to detail, and driving initiatives from strategy through execution. You aren’t afraid to roll up your sleeves, improve processes, and build scalable, repeatable operational solutions that deliver meaningful impact for merchants and the business.

## We’re excited about you because…

- You are a proven operator - You have 2-4 years of experience in consulting / strategy / business development / program management / technology / banking or related experience; high-growth or early-stage startup experience is a plus

- You have a bachelor’s degree or higher 

- You are analytical – You let data tell the story and you're comfortable modeling in Excel. SQL knowledge is a plus.

- You are a collaborator - You have a proven track record of leading initiatives, working cross-functionally, and succeeding in a team first environment

- You are a problem solver – You can independently translate high-level goals into actionable plans.

- You are a strong communicator – You tell the story to different audiences and through different mediums - strong written and verbal communication skills are a must.

We expect this position to be filled by 7/13/26.
