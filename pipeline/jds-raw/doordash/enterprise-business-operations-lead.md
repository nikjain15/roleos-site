# Enterprise Business Operations Lead — DoorDash

## About the Team

The Enterprise & Key Accounts (ENT/KA) team at SevenRooms is responsible for scaling and deepening partnerships with some of the world’s most sophisticated restaurant groups and hospitality operators. As part of DoorDash’s In-Store organization, this team operates at the intersection of enterprise sales, customer success, product, analytics, and revenue operations.

The enterprise business has reached meaningful scale -- with real customers, global complexity, and growing revenue -- while much of the operating infrastructure is still being built. This team is focused on transforming that complexity into a repeatable, scalable operating model that supports long-term growth.

## About the Role

As Lead, Enterprise Business Operations, you will be responsible for building and scaling the operating foundation of the Enterprise & Key Accounts business. You will act as a senior, embedded partner to the Global Head of Enterprise & Key Accounts and work cross-functionally with Sales, Customer Success, Revenue Operations, Analytics, Finance, and Product to translate strategy into execution.

This role sits at the center of ambiguity. You will lead both short-term execution and longer-term operating design -- standing up dashboards, processes, and decision-making frameworks that allow the enterprise business to scale without creating fragility. While this role does not own central RevOps tooling, it plays a critical role in shaping enterprise-specific requirements, prototyping solutions, and helping institutionalize what works.

The ideal candidate is a builder, systems thinker, and operator who thrives in fast-moving environments and enjoys owning outcomes end-to-end.

## You’re excited about this opportunity because you will…

## Build

- Design and stand up the operating model for Enterprise & Key Accounts, including dashboards, tracking mechanisms, and performance cadences

- Translate enterprise GTM strategy into durable systems across pipeline, lead segmentation, whitespace analysis, and account coverage

## Strategize

- Partner with enterprise leadership to define priorities, goals, and success metrics

- Identify the highest-leverage opportunities to improve visibility, efficiency, and execution across the business

## Operate

- Own cross-functional execution on key initiatives spanning Sales, Customer Success, RevOps, Analytics, Finance, and Product

- Act as the connective tissue between strategic intent and day-to-day operations

## Analyze

- Develop and maintain analytical views of enterprise performance using tools like Looker, SQL, Salesforce, and Sheets

- Use data to inform decisions, resolve tradeoffs, and guide leadership discussions

## Scale

- Prototype and pressure-test new processes before partnering with central teams to harden and institutionalize them

- Remove friction from day-to-day execution by building clear ownership, structure, and repeatable processes

## We’re excited about you because…

- You have 8–12+ years of experience in strategy, operations, consulting, finance, BizOps, or a similarly rigorous operating role

- You bring strong analytical depth and are comfortable working directly with data (SQL, Looker, Excel/Sheets)

- You have a bias for action and thrive in ambiguous, fast-paced environments

- You are a systems thinker who enjoys building structure where none exists

- You can operate credibly with senior executives while staying close to operational detail

- You take ownership of outcomes and are comfortable being accountable for both the strategy and the execution

- You are energized by being a generalist and a force multiplier for leadership teams
