# Director Strategy & Operations, Local Markets — DoorDash

## About the Team

Our US Marketplace team is responsible for growing the DoorDash business in the US, helping customers to discover & order what’s best in their city, merchants to grow their sales, and dashers to find flexible work. 

## About the Role

The US Marketplace team is looking for a motivated and hands-on operator to be the Director of Local Markets. As a Strategy & Operations Director, you’ll be responsible for driving marketplace growth in every U.S. market, across a variety of market types.

You will report into the Growth and Markets team, leading cross-functional collaboration between DoorDash’s marketing, sales, product, finance, and analytics teams to deliver new products and service level improvements to drive local market growth. You'll have the opportunity to help define new ways to grow locally, incubate 0-1 experiments, scope partnership opportunities, foster relationships with external partners, and execute on a roadmap to grow the marketplace at the local level. 

## You're excited about this opportunity because you will…

- Lead - From day one you’ll lead a team, helping the team to further develop the muscle of lowest level of detail analyses and execution rigor

- Business development - Develop relationships with new growth partners and top merchants

- Influence - Work cross-functionally across the US Marketplace Organization to help craft roadmaps, secure investment, and partner with other organizations to improve the user experience at local level

- Build – Explore growth opportunities alongside our partners in Product, Marketing, Analytics, Engineering, and Operations teams

- Analyze – Shape and define our strategy based on quantitative and qualitative data, querying data directly and empowering yourself with AI tooling (Cursor, Claude)

- Operate – Figure out how to get from A to B–then actually make it happen.

- Grow – Help the business get 1% better every day.

## We're excited about you because… 

- You have 10+ years of experience in strategy & operations or other operating roles; high-growth startup experience is advantageous

- You have 5+ years of people management experience

- You have experience leading cross-functional projects and hitting goals

- You are comfortable running analysis and manipulating datasets; SQL is required and experience using AI tools (Cursor, Claude) is preferred

- You always look for ways to improve and want to shape the direction of the company

- You can motivate people at all levels across the organization

- You can translate high-level goals into relevant plans

- You love getting to a low level of detail

We expect this position to be filled by 6/15/263.

## About DoorDash

At DoorDash, our mission to empower local economies shapes how our team members move quickly, learn, and reiterate in order to make impactful decisions that display empathy for our range of users—from Dashers to merchant partners to consumers.
