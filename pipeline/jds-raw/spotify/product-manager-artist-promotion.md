# Product Manager - Artist Promotion — Spotify

Spotify for Artists is used by more than 1M active artists and offers a suite of music native marketing products, including Marquee and Showcase. In this role, you will directly shape and improve the value these products deliver to artists. 

From prototyping through development and commercialization, you’ll work with a cross-functional team from Design, Engineering, and Product Insights to define product strategy and ship impact. 

This organization focuses on managing access to the suite, helping artists configure campaigns that drive their unique campaign goals, unlocking new use cases and campaign customizations, and improving performance and delivery by matching listeners and artists.

 

## What You'll Do

- Set the vision and manage the roadmap for a squad within the artist promotion organization 

- Build alignment against your roadmap and gain support from relevant cross-functional teams to deliver impact

- Prioritize new features based on customer needs, business potential, and technical platform scalability

- Set and follow metrics that advise us of the success of the products and their impact on the Spotify ecosystem and users

- Partner with PMs, design, insights, and engineering teams on the design, implementation, delivery and adoption of features

## Who You Are

- 2+ years of experience working as a product manager or similar discipline (i.e. business analytics or strategy, product insights, engineering) in cross-functional teams

- You have experience collaborating with a wide range of non-product partners in a matrixed organization, and understand how to influence and inspire teams

- You’re a compelling storyteller who can communicate in succinct and inspiring ways to a variety of audience types

- You ideally have worked on a B2B or B2C product

- You’re passionate about the opportunity to better serve artists & their teams

- You have a team-first approach and have developed techniques to ensure your teams are happy, motivated and productive

- You are comfortable working with distributed teams across multiple offices and time zones

## Where You'll Be

- This role is based in New York.

- This team operates within the Eastern Standard time zone for collaboration.
