# Alpha TIER Integration Services, Product Lead, Officer — State Street

Who we are looking for

State Street’s Alpha TIER Integration Services team is looking for qualified individuals with strong experience in project management, analysis, workflow design, data analysis, system flows and business architecture in order to create client solutions and execute implementation of the industry’s first ever front-to-back client servicing platform. The Product Manager for Alpha TIER is a senior leader role and candidate must possess skill in complex business data analysis with expertise in requirements gathering, business models and flows, documentation and data mapping; a proven ability to liaise between business/implementation and technology teams; the ability to work with external parties and applications connected to Alpha TIER; and strong collaboration skills.  

State Street Alpha combines our unique capabilities in data and analytics to better serve our clients and build relationship with clients. Through information and insights, investment analytics, data solutions, and front office tools our goal is to help our clients zero in on the opportunities and actions that will generate the most value for the State Street Alpha solution.  We offer client solutions and implementation services that give our clients new functional capabilities that help drive efficiencies and new technical capabilities. This includes performance and risk, investment accounting, compliance, construction, trading, collateral and the data platform and services to support everything across the front and back office.

What you will be responsible for

As Alpha TIER Product Manager you will

- Responsible for designing solutions and onboarding new clients onto State Street’s front-to-back platform
- Lead workflow analysis, data analysis, requirements analysis for current and future state models and architecture
- Lead creation and delivery of internal and client facing on boarding documents (client proposals, playbooks, resource and governance models, cost and revenue models)
- Navigate complex client relationships, build strong business relationships and manage expectations with client stakeholders
- Manage various stages of the on-boarding lifecycle (solution design, integration and testing, implementation and BAU hand over)
- Become a product and platform SME on State Street’s front-to-back platform
- Support business development and sales efforts through consultative client engagement and POC’s as needed
- Provide regular status updates and supporting project management reports as directed
- Drives the data conversion development lifecycle and methodology including requirements analysis, solution design, client acceptance, timeline creation, implementation, unit testing, deployment tools, and maintenance
- Leads issue resolution and gap closure in terms of data provided or discrepancies in data between legacy presentation and the future state model based on the feedback provided

Education & Preferred Qualifications

- Bachelor’s degree or higher.
- 5+ years of financial industry experience across front office functionality along with middle (IBOR) and back office systems & services and Alpha data platform.
- Experience integrating with other industry software providers to promote platform interoperability a plus (i.e. Bloomberg, CRD, BRS Aladdin, etc.)
- Client engagement and expectation management across project life cycle
- Knowledge of SDLC & Agile Methodology and tools (i.e. JIRA) a plus
