## Role Overview
Harvey is looking for a Senior Product Manager, Billing to build and scale the systems that support how enterprise customers understand, trust, and pay for their activity across Harvey’s AI platform. This role sits within the product organization and partners closely with engineering, design, GTM, finance, and legal to bring reliable billing capabilities to market. You will own a critical part of Harvey’s commercial infrastructure. This is a high-impact role for a product leader who can operate at both the customer experience layer and the underlying platform layer as Harvey’s pricing and packaging continue to evolve.

## 

## What You'll Do
- Drive the vision, strategy, and execution for Harvey’s billing infrastructure.

- Partner with engineering and design to build billing capabilities that are accurate, auditable, flexible, and intuitive for enterprise customers.

- Collaborate with go-to-market, finance, legal, sales, and customer success teams to support complex enterprise contracts.

- Shape the customer-facing billing experience, including invoices, reporting, and account-level transparency, while ensuring the underlying infrastructure can scale.

- Help Harvey scale a trusted commercial platform that meets the needs of enterprise customers, internal operators, and financial reporting stakeholders.

## 

## What You Have
- 5+ years of product management experience, with meaningful time spent on billing, platform, or infrastructure.

- Experience building billing, ideally in a SaaS, enterprise software, or AI platform environment.

- Familiarity with third-party billing platforms.

- Strong understanding of the billing stack, including event capture, aggregation, invoicing, revenue recognition, accounting handoffs, and operational controls.

- Excellent stakeholder management skills, with the ability to balance customer needs, commercial priorities, finance requirements, legal considerations, and engineering tradeoffs.

- A systems-thinking approach to product development, with the ability to design architectures and workflows that are reliable, transparent, auditable, and adaptable.
