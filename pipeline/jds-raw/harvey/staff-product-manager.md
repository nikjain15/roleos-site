## Role Overview
At Harvey, we’re transforming how legal and professional services operate – not incrementally, but end-to end. By combining frontier agentic AI, an enterprise-grade platform, and deep domain expertise, we’re reshaping how critical knowledge work gets done for decades to come. As we scale, our Enterprise Product organization sits at the center of this mission — connecting the systems our customers use, and scaling into new customer segments & verticals.

We’re looking for a Product Manager with a strong technical foundation and the ability to own and solve complex product and commercial challenges end-to-end. This person will focus on building out integrations with strategic partners and vendors as well as enable search over data source using MCP’s and deep research. You will help architect systems to bring legal context into Harvey and also work with our Partnerships org and internal teams to prioritize which ones to build. This role is equal parts product intuition and technical execution. You’ll be trusted to make fast, high-quality decisions, ship impactful features, work on the cutting edge of applied AI and grow a team that embodies engineering excellence

We move with urgency, bias toward simplicity, and expect our leaders to be hands-on when needed. If you’re energized by ownership, decisive execution, and solving real user problems, we'd love to hear from you.

This role is based in San Francisco, CA. We use an in-person work model (3 days a week) and offer relocation assistance to new employees.

## What You'll Do
- Lead the end-to-end product lifecycle, from ideation and strategy to execution and launch

- Collaborate closely with engineering and partnerships team to identify and execute on high-impact integrations

- Work closely with engineers to build, platformize and scale integrations, working cross functionally with adjacent teams

- Expand the breadth and depth of integrations into the Harvey platform

- Collaborate cross-functionally with Product Engineering, Applied AI, Research, and Platform teams to deliver high-quality production systems that support reasoning, summarization, and legal research workflows.

## What You Have
- 5+ years of experience building enterprise products in a B2B/commercial environment

- Experience working on Integrations and partnering with cross functional and Partnerships teams

- Experience with or knowledge of AI and LLMs

- Ability to partner with engineers on technical architecture, with enough depth to challenge assumptions, propose solutions, and influence design.

- Ability to navigate extreme ambiguity to deliver outcomes

- A product mindset for balancing user needs, domain complexity, and system constraints to propose high-leverage improvements.
