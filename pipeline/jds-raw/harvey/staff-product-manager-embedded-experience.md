## Role Overview
We're looking for a Staff Product Manager to own and scale Harvey's Embedded Experiences product area—the critical surface where Harvey meets users in their existing workflows. This role is responsible for how Harvey integrates with the productivity tools professionals use every day, including Microsoft Word, Outlook, and emerging copilot experiences.

You'll lead the strategy and execution for our embedded experiences—plugins, add-ins, and native integrations that bring Harvey's AI capabilities directly into the tools our customers already use. You'll define how Harvey becomes an indispensable part of our users' daily workflows, whether they're drafting contracts in Word, managing communications in Outlook, or leveraging AI-assisted copilot experiences across their productivity suite.

This is a highly strategic role at the intersection of platform, product, and partnerships. You'll work closely with engineering, design, partnerships, and GTM teams to build integrations that are not just functional, but transformative—making Harvey the system of intelligence for professional work.

## What You’ll Do
- Own the Product Vision: Define and execute the product strategy for Harvey's embedded experiences, including the Word Add-In, Outlook plugin, and copilot integrations across the Microsoft ecosystem and beyond.

- Integration Prioritization: Work closely with the Integrations engineering pod and Partnerships team to prioritize, scope, and ship integrations that drive customer value and competitive differentiation. Translate partner and customer demand signals into a coherent product roadmap.

- Platform & API Design: Define Harvey's external API strategy and developer experience. Ensure Harvey's platform is extensible and that third-party developers and partners can build on top of it effectively.

- Partner Product Collaboration: Serve as the primary product counterpart for strategic partners (e.g., iManage, LexisNexis, SharePoint). Co-develop integration specifications, align on technical requirements, and ensure seamless product experiences across partner ecosystems.

- Cross-Functional Leadership: Collaborate closely with Engineering (Integrations pod, Platform), Partnerships (Carl Fuda's team), GTM, Customer Success, and PMM to ensure ecosystem investments are aligned with commercial strategy and customer needs.

- Commercial Sense: Partner with GTM and Partnerships to translate ecosystem capabilities into sales enablement, positioning, and customer-facing narratives. Understand how integrations and data partnerships drive deal velocity, retention, and expansion.

- Measurement & Iteration: Define success metrics for ecosystem health (e.g., integration adoption, API usage, partner-driven revenue) and iterate based on data.

What You Have

- 8+ years of product management experience, with at least 3 years at a Staff or Senior PM level at a high-growth technology company.

- Proven track record of owning and scaling complex platform or infrastructure products, ideally in enterprise SaaS, document management, knowledge management, or data-intensive domains.

- Strong technical acumen with the ability to engage deeply with engineering on system design, distributed systems, data pipelines, and retrieval architectures.

- Experience building products that handle sensitive data with robust security, compliance, and access control requirements.

- Excellent communication skills with the ability to influence stakeholders at various levels, from engineers to executives.

- Demonstrated ability to thrive in ambiguous, fast-paced environments and drive clarity through complexity.

- Strong product sense and attention to detail-ability to think through both high-level strategy and nitty-gritty implementation details.
