# Innovation Product Manager — Harvey

## Why Harvey
At Harvey, we’re transforming how legal and professional services operate — not incrementally, but end-to-end. By combining frontier agentic AI, an enterprise-grade platform, and deep domain expertise, we’re reshaping how critical knowledge work gets done for decades to come.

This is a rare chance to help build a generational company at a true inflection point. With 1000+ customers in 60+ countries, strong product-market fit, and world-class investor support, we’re scaling fast and defining a new category in real time. The work is ambitious, the bar is high, and the opportunity for growth — personal, professional, and financial — is unmatched.

Our team is sharp, motivated, and deeply committed to the mission. We move fast, operate with intensity, and take real ownership of the problems we tackle — from early thinking to long-term outcomes. We stay close to our customers — from leadership to engineers — and work together to solve real problems with urgency and care. If you thrive in ambiguity, push for excellence, and want to help shape the future of work alongside others who raise the bar, we invite you to build with us.

At Harvey, the future of professional services is being written today — and we’re just getting started.

## Role Overview
This role blends elements of product management, innovation strategy, solutions design, and forward-deployed partnership. You’ll work directly with leading law firms and corporate legal teams to translate messy, high-value legal work into AI-powered workflows — and ensure they succeed in production.

We don’t build software for lawyers; we build it with them. Our teams sit shoulder-to-shoulder with partners, associates, innovation groups, and CIOs to understand problems at depth, design first-of-kind workflows, and ship products that reshape how legal work gets done.

## What You’ll Do
- Serve as a thought partner to top global law firms and enterprises — diagnosing pain points, mapping high-value opportunities, and guiding how Harvey integrates into their practice.

- Work with the go-to-market team to shape pilots and deployments end-to-end: discovery, workflow design, testing, onboarding, iteration, and firm-wide rollout.

- Work with innovation leaders, legal operations, and practice groups alongside Customer Success — ensuring the platform becomes embedded in day-to-day legal work

- Translate user insights into product direction to influence roadmap

- Help clients evaluate the business impact of AI — from improving associate leverage to transforming workflows across practices like M&A, litigation, regulatory, and transactions.

- Shape how the legal industry adapts to AI. You’ll be in the room where the new operating model of law is being defined.

## What You Have
You come from the legal world, but you’ve always gravitated toward innovation, experimentation, and systems thinking. You’ve seen how complex legal work happens in practice — and you’re excited to redesign it with cutting-edge AI.

You’ll thrive here if you:

- Have experience at a top law firm or in-house legal team; innovation, legal ops, or consulting experience is a plus.

- Familiarity with the legal tech ecosystem as an employee, founder, or advisor to legal tech startups

- Are product-savvy and tech-curious. You’re not expected to code, but you can navigate conversations about generative AI, workflow design, privacy, governance, and enterprise rollout.

- Communicate with clarity and confidence across roles — partners, associates, engineers, PMs, CIOs, and legal ops.

- Can independently run multiple client engagements in parallel.

- Care deeply about quality and user outcomes while keeping a strong bias for action.

- Are energized by building something new in a domain going through its biggest shift in decades.
